2 Nephi 5
24 They used their intellect mischievously to gain influence and power in the land without working with their hands. They became an idle people. They would disturb the harmony of the forest by capturing beasts of prey. They would use these beasts to torment the innocent.
25 God warned me: “They will be a bane to your seed. They will stir up your seed so your seed will remember Me. If your seed does not remember Me (does not listen to Me), I will allow them to scourge your seed even unto destruction.”
26 And I did consecrate Jacob and Joseph. I consecrated them to be priests and teachers over the land of my people.
27 We lived in a happy manner.
28 Thirty years have transpired since we left Jerusalem.
29 I had kept a record of my people I kept these records on my larger plates.
