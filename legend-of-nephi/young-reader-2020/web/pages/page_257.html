2 Nephi 13
11 “Wo to the wicked. They will perish. For the reward of their hands will be upon them!”
12 “My people will be oppressed by children. Women will rule over them.”
13 “God will stand. God will urge them to be righteous. God will judge the people in righteousness.”
14 “God will reason with the ancients of His people. God will castigate with the princes:” “You have destroyed the vineyard. You have plundered from the houses of the poor.”
15 “Why have you afflicted My people? Why have oppressed the poor with such contempt?” “God will make the unjust answer these charges.”
16 “Moreover, God said:” “The daughters of Zion are haughty. A daughter of Zion walks with stretched-forth necks. She looks with wanton eyes. She walks with small steps as she goes, making a tinkling with her feet.”
