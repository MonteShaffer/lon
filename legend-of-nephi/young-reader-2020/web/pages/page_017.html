1 Nephi 3
13 Laban became angry. Laban told Laman he could not have the family history. Laban told Laman to get out of his house. Laban accused Laman of being a robber and threatened to kill Laman.
14 Laman ran away from Laban and returned to us (brothers). Upon hearing this story, we were all dejected. My brothers wanted to return to my father in the wilderness.
15 I said to them: “As God lives, and as we live, we will not return to our father in the wilderness until we complete this task.”
16 “Let us be faithful to God. [I have an idea.] Let’s go to the land of our father’s inheritance. Remember, God told him to leave behind the gold, silver and other precious things.”
17 “Remember, father knew that Jerusalem is going to be destroyed because of the wickedness of the people.”
