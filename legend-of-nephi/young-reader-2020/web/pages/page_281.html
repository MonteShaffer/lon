2 Nephi 18
17 “And I will wait for God. God that hides His face from the house of Jacob. Yet I will look for Him.”
18 “My sons and I are signs of God’s wonders. Yes the Lord of Hosts who dwells in Mount Zion.”
19 “The peoples of Israel will say to you:” “Seek necromancers that whisper. Seek soothsayer that meditate.” “And I say: Should not a people seek their living God to hear from the dead?”
20 “This is the instruction and the testimony. One must speak about this matter in this manner. Otherwise there is no light in him.”
21 “Many will be distressed with troubles. They will be hungry. When they are hungry, they will worry. They will look upward. They will curse their God and king.”
22 “And they will look to the earth and behold trouble and darkness. Yes, gloom of distress. They will be thrust into darkness.”
