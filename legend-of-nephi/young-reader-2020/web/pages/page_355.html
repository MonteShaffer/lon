2 Nephi 29
7 “Know you not that there are more nations than one? Know you not that I, the Lord your God, have created all men? That I remember those who are upon the isles of the sea. That I rule in the heavens above and in the earth below the heavens. That I bring forth My word unto the children of men. Yes, even to all the nations of the earth.”
8 “You murmur because you don’t want more of My word? Know you not that two testimonies are better than one? The testimony of two nations witness that I am God. Why would I not remember one nation like another? Wherefore, I speak the same words to one nation like another. When the two nations will run together, the testimony of the two nations will also run together.”
