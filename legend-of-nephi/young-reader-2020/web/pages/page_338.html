2 Nephi 27
13 “And no one else will view this book. Except for maybe a few more witnesses, as God commands. In this, God’s words will be fulfilled. He promised that the words of the faithful would speak as if it were from the dead.”
14 “Wherefore, God will initiate the bringing forth of the words of the book. God will determine how many witnesses He wants to testify to establish His word, as written in this book. And wo be to those that reject the word of God, as written in this book.”
15 “Behold, God will tell this man (the unlearned) who received the book:” “Take a sample of the writing of the unsealed portion of the book, and give it to a private courier. This courier will show the writing sample to to learned saying:” “Please read this.” “And the learned will say:” “Bring me the entire book, and I will.”
