1 Nephi 20
17 “The Eternal Father declares:” “I have sent Jehovah. Jehovah is the Holy One of Israel. He is your Redeemer. He will teach you to improve yourselves. He will show you the path you should go because He has taken that path Himself.”
18 “And Jehovah declares:” “I wish you had listened to My commandments. If you had, your peace would flow as a river. Your righteousness would be constant as the waves of the sea.”
19 “If you had, your seed would have been as numerous as the sands of the sea. If you had, the offspring of your bowels would have been as numerous as the gravel of the river. If you had, you would have avoided being destroyed. You would have avoided being cut off from the promises made to Jacob.”
