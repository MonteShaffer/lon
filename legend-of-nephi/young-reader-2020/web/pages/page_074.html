1 Nephi 13
26 “The twelve apostles of the Lamb of God wrote many things that were originally included in the book. After the apostles wrote these things, the great and abominable church was formed. This church is the most abominable of all other churches. This church took away many parts of the gospel of the Lamb of God from this book. Many plain and precious truths were removed from the book. Many of God’s covenants were also removed from the book.”
27 “Leaders of this abominable church removed these things with purpose. They wanted to pervert God’s truths. They wanted to blind the eyes and harden the hearts of the children of men.”
28 “The book you see is the true book of the Lamb of God after it passed through the hands of the great and abominable church.”
