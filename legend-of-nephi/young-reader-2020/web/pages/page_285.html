2 Nephi 19
17 “God will have no joy in their young men. Neither will God have mercy for their fatherless and widows. For every one of them is a hypocrite and an evildoer. Every mouth speaks senselessness. God’s anger is not turned away. His hand is stretched out still.”
18 “The wicked will burn by fire. This fire will devour the briers and thorns. It will kindle in the thickets of the forests. A raging column of smoke will billow forth.”
19 “Through God’s wrath the land will darken. People will be the fuel that feeds the burning fire. No champion will spare his brother.”
20 “The champion will seize the substance of his neighbor on the right and be hungry. The champion will seize the substance of his neighbor on the left and not be satisfied. Every man will trust in his own strength. By this strength he will consume or be consumed.”
