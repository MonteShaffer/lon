1 Nephi 8
7 “And I followed him. I was in a dark and dreary wilderness.”
8 “After I had traveled for many hours in the dark, I began to pray. I prayed that God would have mercy on me. I know God can be compassionate.”
9 “After I prayed to God, I arrived at a large and spacious field.”
10 “I also saw a fruit-bearing tree. I perceived that the fruit was desirable. I perceived that eating the fruit would make one happy.”
11 “I walked towards the tree. I ate the fruit. It was very sweet. It was sweeter than any fruit I ever had tasted before. The fruit was white in color. It was whiter than anything I had ever seen before.”
