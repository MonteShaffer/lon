1 Nephi 15

1 I (Nephi) had been carried away by the Spirit. I saw many things. After this vision, I returned to the tent of my father.
2 I saw my brothers discussing the things my father had shared with them. They were arguing about the meaning of these things.
3 Truly, Lehi shared many great things with them. Things that are difficult to understand. A man can understand these things by asking God. But my brothers had hardened their hearts. They did not look to God for an understanding of these things.
4 I was again grieved to see this. My brothers had hardened their hearts. I had seen in my vision that results of such wickedness. Not just with my brothers, but with all of the children of men.
