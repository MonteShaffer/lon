1 Nephi 17
9 And I replied: “God, I need tools to build the ship as You showed me. Where should I go to find the ore? I will need to craft tools with molten ore.”
10 And God told me where would find ore. With this ore, I could make tools.
11 I [prepared a process to melt ore]. I made bellows from animal hides. After I had prepared [my furnace], I made fire. I struck two stones together to make fire.
12 As we journeyed in the wilderness, we had not used fire. God had not allowed us to use fire. God explained: “I will make your food sweet so you don’t have to cook it.”
13 God had also promised: “I will be your light in the wilderness. I will prepare the path before you. I will do so if you keep my commandments. If you keep my commandments, I will lead you toward that promised land. And you will know that you are led and preserved because of Me.”
