1 Nephi 13
7 I also saw gold, silver, silks, scarlets, fine-twined linen, and all manner of precious clothing. I also saw many harlots.
8 And the angel spoke: “Behold these things are the desires of this great and abominable church.”
9 “Worldly power motivates those in the abominable church. They seek praise of the world. This is why they kill the saints of God. This is why they enslave the saints of God.”
10 And I looked. I saw many waters. And these waters divided the Gentiles from the seed of my brothers.
11 And the angel spoke: “Behold the wrath of God! It will descend upon the seed of your brothers.”
12 And I looked. I saw a man among the Gentiles. The Spirit of God descended upon this man. He traveled from the land of the Gentiles to this promised land. He traveled across the many waters to the seed of my brothers.
