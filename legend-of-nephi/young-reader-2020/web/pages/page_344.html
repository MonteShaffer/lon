2 Nephi 28

1 Now behold my brothers, I have spoken these things to you. I have spoken as prompted by the Spirit. Wherefore, I know that these things will come to pass.
2 The things which will be written out of this book will be of great worth to the children of men. They will have special value to our seed, which is a remnant of the house of Israel.
3 “In that day, many churches will be established. They are not God’s church, but each will claim to be. One will say:” “Behold this is God’s church.” “ And another will say:” “No, this is God’s church.” “In that day, everyone will say that have established God’s church, but none are God’s church.”
