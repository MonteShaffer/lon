2 Nephi 18
6 “This people refuse the waters of Shiloah that go softly. This people rejoice in Rezin and Remaliah’s son.”
7 “Therefore, God will bring strong waters of a river. Yes, many will come from the king of Assyria. You will see this king’s glory as he will come from all channels and possess all banks.”
8 “And Assyria’s glory will not stop with Syria and Samaria. It will overflow and go into Judah. It will reach even the great city of Jerusalem. And the stretching out of Assyria’s wings will fill the breadth of your land, O Immanuel.”
9 “Tremble, O mighty ones! You will be broken to pieces. Listen all the countries of the earth. Prepare yourselves! You will be broken to pieces. Yes, prepare yourselves! You will be broken to pieces.”
10 “The mighty will conspire together. To no avail. The mighty will speak. To no avail. For God is with us.”
