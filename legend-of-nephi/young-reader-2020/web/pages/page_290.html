2 Nephi 20
15 “How the ax boasts itself to He who swings it! How the saw praises itself to He who cuts with it! As if the scepter can brandish itself against He who holds it. As if the wooden staff can exalt itself if He did not create wood!”
16 “Therefore, God, the Lord of Hosts, will make the robust soldiers of this king waste away. For his arrogance, God will kindle a fire that destroys Assyria.”
17 “God will be the light of Israel. The pure in heart will be the tip of His flaming sword. They will kindle a fire that will consume the thorns and briers in one day.”
18 “And the majestic honeycombs will burn. All of his glory will be destroyed. The fruitful fields. Both the bodies and souls of men. And the royal banner will be no more.”
19 “Those that survive this destruction will be few. So few that a child can count them.”
