2 Nephi 7
4 “God has given me the tongue of the learned. He did so that I may weary you with God’s word O house of Israel. Every morning, God stirs me with ideas. I listen and learn even more.”
5 “God has opened my ear. I was not rebellious. I did not backtrack from Him.”
6 “I gave my back to Him. I was beaten by haters. I gave my cheeks to Him. I was struck by haters. I gave my beard to Him. The haters pulled out my beard. Yet I did not hide my face in shame even when they did spit upon me.”
7 “God will help me. I will not be confounded. I have set my face as flint. I know that I am not ashamed.”
8 “God is near. God justifies me. Who then, will contend with me? Let us stand together. Who is mine adversary? Let him come near me. I will smite him with the strength of my mouth.”
