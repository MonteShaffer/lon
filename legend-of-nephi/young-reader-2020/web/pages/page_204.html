2 Nephi 5
18 And the people wanted me to be their king. But I did not want the people be ruled by a king. Nevertheless, I did for them according to that which was in my power and they considered me their king.
19 God’s words to my brothers were fulfilled. I was a ruler and teacher. And previously, I had been the ruler and teacher over my brothers. As God had commanded me. Until they tried to kill me so we separated ourselves from them.
20 God’s word was fulfilled. He told me: “If your brothers will not listen to your words, they will be cut off from My presence.” And it happened. My brothers abandoned their beliefs of the true living God.
