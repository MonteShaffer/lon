1 Nephi 10
4 Six hundred years after Lehi left Jerusalem, God will send a prophet to the Jewish nation in their land of inheritance. This prophet will be the Messiah, a Savior of the world.
5 Lehi taught us that a great number of prophets had testified that a Messiah would come. This Messiah would redeem this world.
6 Lehi taught that all mankind are in a lost and fallen state. They need a Redeemer to save them from this state.
7 Lehi spoke about another prophet that would precede the Messiah. This prophet will prepare the way for the Messiah.
8 This prophet will go into the wilderness and preach: “Prepare the way for the coming of the Messiah. Make a righteous path for Him. He will stand among you and you will not know him. He is mightier than I. I am not worthy to loosen the strap of His sandals.” Lehi said much about this prophet.
