2 Nephi 13

1 “For God, the Lord of Hosts, will punish them. Yes He will punish Jerusalem. He will punish Judah. He will remove all sustenance. Little bread will be found. Little water will be found.”
2 “[God will remove Judah’s leadership.] The mighty man. The man of war. The judge. The prophet. The prudent. The ancient.”
3 “The captain of fifty. The honorable man. The counselor. The skillful artisan. The eloquent orator.”
4 “And God will give them children to be their princes. And God will give them babes to rule over them.”
5 “People will become greedy in their oppression. Every one against another. Every one against his neighbor. The child will show disdain for the ancients. The debased will despise the honorable.”
