2 Nephi 27
6 “And God will bring forth the words of a book to them. These words will contain the writings of those who slumber.”
7 “Behold, a portion of the book will be sealed. In this sealed portion will be comprehensive revelation from God. Yes, it will reveal all things. From the beginning of the world. To the ending of the world.”
8 “This sealed portion will be kept from the people. Because of their wickedness and abominations. During their wicked days, these things will not be delivered. Because of how these things can be misused in days of wickedness.”
9 “This book will be delivered to a man. This man will deliver some of the words of the book. The words of those who have slumbered in the dust. He will deliver these words to another.”
