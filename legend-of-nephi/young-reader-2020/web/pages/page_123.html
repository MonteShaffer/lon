1 Nephi 18

1 My brothers repented. They worshipped God. And they went forth with me and helped me with the work. And we did work the timbers in a novel way. From time to time, God would show details about how I should work the timbers of the ship.
2 I did not work the timbers after the manner of men. I worked the timbers as God showed me. I did not build the ship after the manner of men. I built the ship as God showed me.
3 I ascended to the mountain often. There, I would pray often to God. God would show me great things.
4 After the ship was finished, my brothers saw that it was good. They observed that the ship was of a superior quality. As a result, they humbled themselves before God. The knew this unique design came from God.
5 God spoke to my father. God commanded that we should pack up and enter the ship.
