2 Nephi 17
9 “The capital of Ephraim is Samaria. And Remaliah’s son is the king of Samaria. Within sixty-five years Ephraim will be broken. It will not be a people any longer. Therefore king Ahaz, believe my words so that God’s will be done.”
10 “Isaiah continued speaking to Ahaz (on God’s behalf), saying:”
11 “Ask God for a sign. Ask for a sign from the deep below. Or ask for a sign from the exalted above.”
12 “And Ahaz answered:” “I will not doubt God’s power.”
13 And Isaiah answered: “Listen to me O house of David. It is easy for men to be offended. But will you also offend my God ?”
14 “Therefore, God will give himself as a the miraculous sign. That is, a virgin will conceive. She will bear a son. And the son will be proclaimed to be the renown Immanuel.”
