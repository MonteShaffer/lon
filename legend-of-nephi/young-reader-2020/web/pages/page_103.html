1 Nephi 16
19 We returned to our families without food. Everyone was hungry. Everyone was weary from travel.
20 And the murmuring commenced. Laman and Lemuel and the sons of Ishmael complained exceedingly. They complained about the suffering and afflictions of the wilderness. My father joined in. He murmured against God. Everyone was upset. Everyone was complaining. Everyone wanted food.
21 And the other men complained that their bows had lost their springs. They grumbled that it was too difficult to hunt with their bows. They did torment me with their complaining.
22 I called the others out for complaining. I let them know that they were complaining against God. They were hardening their hearts again.
23 Instead of complaining, I made a bow out of wood. I also took a straight stick and made an arrow. I armed myself with this bow and arrow. I also armed myself with a sling and stones. And I asked my father: “Where should I go to obtain food?”
