2 Nephi 2
28 “And now, my sons, I want you to look to the great Mediator (the Messiah). Listen to God’s great commandments. Be faithful to God’s words. Choose eternal life, according to the will of Holy Spirit.”
29 “Don’t choose eternal death, according to the will of the flesh. Evil can dwell in the flesh. This gives the spirit of the wicked one power to captivate. The power to bring you down to hell. The wicked one wants to reign over you in his own kingdom.”
30 ‘I (Lehi) spoke these words to all of you my sons. These are the last days of my probation. I have chose the good part. According to the words of the prophet. My only remaining concern is the everlasting welfare of your souls. Amen.”
