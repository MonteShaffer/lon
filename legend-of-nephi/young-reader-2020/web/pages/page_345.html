2 Nephi 28
4 “And they will contend with each other. Their priests will contend with each other. They will teach their own learning and deny the Holy Ghost. Yes, the Holy Ghost which transmits truth.”
5 “They deny the power of God. They deny the Holy One of Israel. They say unto the people:” “Listen to us. Hear our wisdom. For there is no prophecies today. The Redeemer has done His work. He has given His power to men.”
6 “Behold, listen to our philosophies. If one claims a miracle of God has occurred, believe it not. Today, God is not a God of miracles. He has done His work.”
7 “Yes, and others will say:” “Eat, drink, and be merry! For tomorrow we die. And it will be well with us.”
