1 Nephi 14
10 And the angel said: “Behold, there are only two churches. One is the church of the Lamb of God. The other is the church of the wicked one. If you do not belong to the church of the Lamb of God, this means you belong to the other church. The other great church is the mother of abominations and is the whore of all the earth.”
11 And I looked and saw this church. She sat upon many waters. She had dominion over all the earth. She was the whore of the entire earth. She had dominion over all nations, kindreds, tongues, and people. Her numbers were great.
12 And I also looked and saw the church of the Lamb of God. Its numbers were few because of the wicked abominations of the wicked one’s church. The members of the church of the Lamb of God were few, but they were spread throughout the world. Their dominions were small because the dominion of the wicked one’s church was so great. Members of the church of the Lamb of God were called saints of God.
