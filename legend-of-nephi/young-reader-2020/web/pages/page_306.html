2 Nephi 24
10 “And they will respond to the arrival of the wicked one:” “Have you also become weak? Are you now like us?”
11 “The magnificence of the wicked one one has brought him down to hell. The noise of his instrument is heard no more. His bed is made of maggots. His only coverings are his crimson robes.”
12 “How far have you fallen, O day-star, son of the morning! You that weakened the nations, now down in hell!”
13 “For you had said in your heart:” “I will ascend in the heavens. I will exalt my throne above the stars of God. I will hold counsel in the northern side of the mountain.”
14 “I will climb to the top of the stairs. I will be like the Most High.”
15 “Look at yourself now! You have been thrust down to the lowest pits of hell.”
