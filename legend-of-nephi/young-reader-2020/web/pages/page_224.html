2 Nephi 9
4 “For I know you have searched much. Many of you wanted to know about the things to come. I know that you know that our flesh must waste away and die. Nevertheless, in our bodies we will see God.”
5 “Yes, I know that in the body, God will show himself to those in Jerusalem. The place we came from. Of all possible places, it is expedient that He should be among them. The great Creator found it necessary that He suffered himself. He became subject to man in the flesh. He should die for all men. He did this that all men might be subject to Him.”
6 “All men must die. To fulfill the merciful plan of the great Creator, men must live again. Therefore, there must be a power of resurrection. The resurrection is needed because of the fall of man. And the fall came about because of the transgression (of Adam and Eve). Because mankind was fallen they were cut off from God’s presence.”
