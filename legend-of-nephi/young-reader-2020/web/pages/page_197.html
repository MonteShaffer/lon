2 Nephi 4
23 God has heard my cries by day. God has given me knowledge by visions in the night-time.
24 By day I wax bold in mighty prayer to God. Yes, my voice I have sent up to the heavens. And the angels have come down and ministered to me.
25 On the wings of God’s Spirit, my body has been carried away to exceedingly high mountains. My eyes have seen great things. Yes, things too great for man. Therefore, God has compelled me not to write them down.
26 I have seen such great things. I have seen the condescension of God to the children of men. I have seen God visit men with His great mercy. “Why should my heart weep? Why does my soul linger in the valley of sorrow? Why does my flesh waste away? Why does my strength weaken? All because of my afflictions.”
27 “Why should I yield to sin because of my flesh? Yes, why should I give way to temptations? The temptations give the evil one place in my heart to destroy my peace and afflict my soul? Why am I angry because of my enemy?”
