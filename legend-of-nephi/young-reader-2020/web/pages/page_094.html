1 Nephi 15
20 And I did recite many of the words of Isaiah to my brothers. Isaiah explained how the Jews (more precisely the house of Israel) would be restored. They would not longer be confounded, suppressed, or scattered again. I explained many things to my brothers. They seemed pacified and content with my response. They humbled themselves before God.
21 Later, my brothers returned with more questions: “What did our father’s vision mean? What does the tree mean that he saw in this vision?”
22 And I answered: “The tree is a representation of the tree of life.”
23 And my brothers asked another question: “What does the rod of iron mean that led to the tree?”
