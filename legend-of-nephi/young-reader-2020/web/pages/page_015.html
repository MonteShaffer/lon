1 Nephi 3

1 I (Nephi) returned from speaking to God. I entered the tent of my father.
2 Lehi spoke to me, saying: “I have had a dream. God has told me that you and your brothers need to return to Jerusalem.”
3 “Laban has the record of the Jews and also our family genealogy. These things are engraven upon pages made of a brass alloy (brass plates).”
4 “God told me that you and your brothers need to go to Laban’s house. Get the family history. Bring it back to me here in the wilderness.”
5 “Your brothers are complaining saying this is too difficult a task that I require. But I don’t require it. God requires it.”
6 “Nephi, go my son. Because you don’t complain, God will give you success!”
