2 Nephi 1
8 “For now, other nations will not know about this land. This is God’s wisdom. Once they see it, many nations will overrun it. And there would be no place for your inheritance.”
9 “I (Lehi) have been promised by God. God promised all those He brings from Jerusalem will prosper. If they keep God’s commandments, they will prosper on the face of the land. This land will be theirs alone to possess. Knowledge of this land will be theirs alone. If they keep God’s commandments, they will be blessed on the face of this land. None will molest them. None will take away the land of their inheritance. They will dwell safely forever.”
