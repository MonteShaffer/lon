1 Nephi 13
17 The mother nations and kingdoms gathered together to battle against the liberated Gentiles living in the promised land. They gathered forces upon the many waters. They gathered forces upon the land.
18 I saw the power of God was with the liberated Gentiles living in the promised land. The wrath of God descended upon the mother nations and kingdoms gathered together to battle against them.
19 I saw that God was with the liberated Gentiles. Delivered by the power of God, they defeated all of their enemies.
20 I saw that these people did prosper in the land. And I saw that they carried a book with them.
21 And the angel asked: “Do you understand what this book is?”
22 And I answered: “I do not.”
