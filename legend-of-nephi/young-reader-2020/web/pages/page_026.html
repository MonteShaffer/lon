1 Nephi 4
28 When Laman saw me, he thought I was Laban. He was very afraid. Lemuel and Sam were also very afraid. My brothers began to run away. They thought I was Laban. They thought Laban had killed me and now were coming to kill them.
29 I called to my brothers. They understood it was me. They stopped running away.
30 The servant then recognized my brothers. He realized they were not the elders of the Jews. He began to quiver with fear. He was about to run away and return to the city of Jerusalem.
31 (I am a large man in stature. God has given me natural strength.) I grabbed the servant of Laban and held him, preventing him from running away.
32 I talked to the servant. I told him that if he would listen to me and listen to God I would not kill him.
33 I swore an oath: “As I live and as God lives, I promised you will be a free man if you come with us into the wilderness.”
