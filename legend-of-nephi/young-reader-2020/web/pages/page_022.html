1 Nephi 4
3 “Brothers, you know this story is true. Why then do you doubt now? An angel just told you God will help us. Let’s go and get this done. God will deliver us to destroy Laban as God delivered our fathers to destroy the Egyptians.”
4 After saying these things, my brothers calmed a bit. But they were still angry. And they continued murmuring. However, they did follow me back to Jerusalem. We stopped just outside the walls of the city.
5 It was night. I told my brothers to hide outside the city walls. After they hid, I snuck into the city. I headed towards the house of Laban.
6 I was led by the Spirit. I did not know beforehand what I was about to do.
7 I approached the house of Laban. As I got near, I saw a drunk man slumbering on the ground.
8 I recognized the man. It was Laban!
