1 Nephi 12
8 And the angel spoke: “Behold the twelve disciples of the Lamb of God! They are chosen to minister to your seed.”
9 And the angel said: “Remember the twelve apostles of the Lamb of God? They will judge the twelve tribes of Israel. The twelve ministers of your seed will also be judged by the twelve apostles. You and your seed are also descendants of the house of Israel.”
10 “These twelve ministers of your seed will judge your seed. They are righteous forever. Because of their faith in the Lamb of God, their garments are made white in His blood.”
11 And the angel spoke: “Look!” And I looked. I saw three generations pass in righteousness. Their garments were white even like the garments of the Lamb of God. And the angel said: “Because of their faith in the Lamb of God, their garments are made white in His blood.”
