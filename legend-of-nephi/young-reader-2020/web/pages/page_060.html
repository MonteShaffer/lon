1 Nephi 11
27 And I looked. And I saw the Redeemer of the world my father had talked about. I also saw the prophet that would prepare the way for Him. This prophet baptized the Lamb of God. After He was baptized, I saw the heavens open. The Holy Ghost descended from heaven (in the form of a dove) and dwelled with Him.
28 I saw the Lamb of God minister to the people with power and great glory. Multitudes gathered together to listen to Him. I also saw that some cast Him out of their presence.
29 I also saw twelve others who followed Him. These twelve were carried away in the Spirit. I looked toward them, but they were gone. They had disappeared from my presence.
30 Again the angel spoke: “Look!” And I looked. I saw the heavens open again. I saw angels descending from heaven to minister to the children of men.
