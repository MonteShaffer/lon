2 Nephi 4
28 “Awake my soul! Don’t droop in sin. Rejoice, my heart! Don’t give a place anymore for the enemy of my soul.”
29 “Don’t be angry again because of my enemies. Don’t get discouraged because of mine afflictions.”
30 “Rejoice, my heart!” Cry unto God and say: “O God, I will praise You forever. Yes my soul will rejoice in you, my God, the rock of my salvation.”
31 “O God, will you redeem my soul? Will You deliver me out of the hands of my enemies? Will you cause me to tremble at the appearance of sin?”
32 “May the gates of hell be shut forever before me. My heart is broken. My spirit is contrite. O God, please do not shut the gates of Your righteousness before me. Bless me that I may walk in the path of the low valley. That I may be confined to the plain road!”
