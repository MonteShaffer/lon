2 Nephi 28
8 “Yes, and others will say:” “Eat, drink, and be merry! Nevertheless fear God. God will justify committing a little sin. Yes, lie a little. Take advantage of one because of God’s words. Dig a pit for your neighbor. There is no harm in this. Enjoy all things. For tomorrow we die. And if it be we are guilty, God will beat us with a few stripes. In the end, we will be saved in the kingdom of God.”
9 “Yes, many will teach in this manner. These are false, vain, and foolish doctrines. Those preaching such things will be puffed up in their hearts. They will conceal their profound skill from God! They will work deeds in the darkness.”
10 “And the blood of the saints will cry from the ground against them.”
11 “They have all strayed from the path. They have become corrupted.”
