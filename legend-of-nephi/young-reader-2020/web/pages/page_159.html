1 Nephi 22
18 “Brothers, these things will happen in God’s time. They will happen to men in the flesh. Blood, fire, and vapor of smoke must come. It will fill the earth. And the wicked will burn. They will burn because they harden their hearts against God.”
19 “Anyone that fights against Zion will be destroyed by fire. And the righteous will be preserved.”
20 “God will prepare a prophet to preserve His people. Remember the words of Moses: ” “The Eternal Father will send the great prophet. Every word this great prophet speaks will be fulfilled. If you listen to this prophet, you will be counted among God’s people.”
21 “Brothers, let me tell you who Moses referred to as the great prophet. The great prophet is the Messiah. The Holy One of Israel. [Jehovah will take upon flesh. He will carry out God’s will.] He will execute judgment in righteousness.”
