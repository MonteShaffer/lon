2 Nephi 8
5 “Victory is near. Salvation has gone forth. My arm will judge the people. The isles will wait for Me. They will trust in My arm.”
6 “Lift your eyes to the heavens. Now look down to the earth below you. Ask yourself, which of these created you? Those of the earth will die like a gnat, but those of heaven will live forever. My salvation will be forever. My victory will never fail.”
7 “Listen to Me, you that know righteousness. In your hearts I have written My law. Fear not the reproach of men. Do not be afraid of their revilings. You are My people.”
8 “Those that revile you will be eaten up, like a moth eats a garment. Like a worm eats wool. But My victory will be forever. My salvation is for all generations of time and for all eternity.”
9 “Wake up! Wake up! Put on strength, O arm of God. Awake as in the ancient days. Are You not He that cut Rehob ? Yes, did You not slay this vile sea monster?”
