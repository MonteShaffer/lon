2 Nephi 5
30 God commanded me: “Make other plates. On these (smaller) plates engrave spiritual matters. Things I would declare good. That these writings may be for a profit to your people.”
31 I obeyed God’s command. I went and made these (smaller) plates. I am writing the spiritual things on them.
32 I wrote down what I esteem to be pleasing to God. If my people are pleased with things of God. They will be pleased with the writings on these plates.
33 If my people wants to know details about our history. They particulars can be found if they search my (larger) plates.
34 It suffices me to say that now forty years have transpired since we left Jerusalem. We have had wars and contentions with our enemies the Lamanites.
