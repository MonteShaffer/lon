2 Nephi 23
12 “I will make a man more precious than pure gold. A man will be more precious than the fine gold of Ophir.”
13 “Therefore I will move the heavens. I will move earth from her place.” “This will happen in the day of God’s fierce anger. The day of the wrath of the Lord of Hosts.”
14 “And the earth shall be driven away in glory. As sheep assemble to their shepherd, so too will noble men turn towards their kinsman. They will disappear to their own districts.”
15 “Everyone encountered will be pierced. Everyone caught will prostrate before the sword.”
16 “Their children will be dashed to pieces before their eyes. Their houses will be plundered. Their wives will be ravished.”
17 “Behold I will rouse the middle part against them. This middle part will not esteem silver and gold because they don’t find pleasure in it.”
