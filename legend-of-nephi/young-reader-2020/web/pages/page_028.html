1 Nephi 5

1 My father was filled with joy to see us when we arrived. My mother, Sariah, was also very pleased. She had, in fact, mourned because of us.
2 She had supposed that we had died in the wilderness. She had murmured against my father. She told Lehi that he was a visionary man. She said: “Lehi, you have led us away from the land of our inheritance. My sons are dead. And now, we are dying in the wilderness.”
3 This is how my mother complained against my father.
4 My father responder to her: “Yes, I am a visionary man. If I had not seen the things of God in a vision, I would not have known of the goodness of God. I would have stayed in Jerusalem. I would have died.”
