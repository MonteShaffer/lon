1 Nephi 15
28 And I explained the awful gulf that separated the wicked from the tree of life. That separated the wicked from the saints of God.
29 I explained that the gulf represented the awful hell. I explained what the angel taught me about this hell. It was prepared for the wicked. It was a great pit.
30 I also explained that our father saw the justice of God. Like a flaming [sword] it will divide the wicked from the righteous. Justice is like an eternal flaming fire. Forever, it ascends to God and has no end.
31 And my brothers asked another question: “What exactly is this torment? Is it a temporal torment, while one is in a physical body? Or is it the final state of the soul after the death of the physical body?”
