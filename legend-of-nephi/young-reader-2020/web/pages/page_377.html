2 Nephi 33
11 “And if you do not believe these words to be the words of the Messiah, be forewarned! At the last day, you and I will stand face-to-face before God’s judgment bar. The Messiah will show you, with power and great glory, that these words are His words. And you will know that the Messiah had instructed me to write these things, notwithstanding my weakness.”
12 “And I pray to the Father in the name of the Messiah. I pray that many of us, if not all of us, may be saved in His kingdom in that great and last day.”
13 And now my beloved brothers. And all those of the house of Israel. And all ends of the earth that receive these things. I speak to you as the voice of one crying from the dust: “Farewell until that great and last day will come.”
