1 Nephi 13
38 I (Nephi) saw the remnant of the seed of my brothers. I also saw the book of the Lamb of God. The Gentiles shared this book with the remnant of the seed of my brothers.
39 I saw other books. These books also came forth by the power of God. The Gentiles shared these books with the remnant of the seed of my brothers. God will bring forth many records to convince the Jews, the Gentiles, and the remnant of the seed of my brothers. God wants to convince them that the words of the prophets and twelve apostles are true.
