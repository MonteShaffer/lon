1 Nephi 13
13 I also saw the Spirit of God descend upon other Gentiles. God inspired them to travel across the many waters to this land of promised. These Gentiles traveled to escape subjection from the kingdoms of the Gentiles.
14 I saw many multitudes of Gentiles living in the land of promise. I saw the wrath of God descend upon the seed of my brothers. I saw the Gentiles scatter the seed of my brothers. I saw the Gentiles kill many of the seed of my brothers.
15 And I saw the Spirit of God was with the Gentiles. God blessed them with prosperity. The Gentiles obtained this promised land as their land of inheritance. I saw that the Gentiles were white. I saw that the Gentiles were exceedingly fair and beautiful. This countenance was similar to my people before they perished.
16 I saw the Gentiles escape subjection. These liberated Gentiles were humbled by their freedom and prosperity. They humbled themselves before God. And the power of God was with them.
