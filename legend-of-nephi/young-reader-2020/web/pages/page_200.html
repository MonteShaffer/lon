2 Nephi 5

1 I (Nephi) did cry to God in prayer. Because of the anger of my brothers.
2 Their anger did increase against me. My brothers did seek to kill me.
3 Yes, my brothers did murmur against me saying: “Our younger brother thinks he will rule over us. We have had many tribulations because of him. Let’s kill him. Let’s kill him so he can’t afflict us any more with his words. Behold, we will not have him to be our ruler. Rights of ruling belong to us. We are the older brothers. We should rule over this people.”
4 Now I don’t write everything on these small plates they said. Suffice me to say, my brothers wanted to kill me.
