2 Nephi 16
10 “This people harden their hearts. They close their ears. They shut their eyes. They do not want to see with their eyes. They do not want to hear with their ears. They do not want to understand with their hearts. They do not want to be converted. They do not want to be healed.”
11 “Then I asked: God, how long ?” And God answered:” “Until the cities crash into ruins without inhabitants. Until the homes are without man. Until the whole earth will be utterly crashed into ruins.”
12 “God has been moved men far away. There will be a great desertion of homes in all ends of the earth.”
13 “But yet a tenth will remain. They will return to their country. They will be burned. Yet like a teil tree. Yet like an oak. Even when scorched, a holy seed will come forth. Even when cut to a stump, a holy seed will come forth.”
