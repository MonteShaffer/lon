2 Nephi 10
21 “Great are the promises of God. He promised many on the isles of the sea. And since He says isles (plural), there must be other inhabitable lands. And they may be inhabited by our brothers of the house of Israel.”
22 “Behold, from time to time, God leads away some from the house of Israel. This is according to God’s will and pleasure. Certainly, God remembers all those He has broken off. He also remembers us.”
23 “Cheer up your hearts. Remember you are free to act for yourselves. You can choose the way of everlasting death. Or you can choose the way of eternal life.”
24 “My beloved brothers. Reconcile yourselves to God’s will. Do not reconcile yourselves to the will of the wicked one. Do not reconcile yourselves to the will of the flesh. Remember that only through God’s benevolence are you saved. So align yourself with God.”
