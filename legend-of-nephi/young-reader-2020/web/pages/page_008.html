1 Nephi 1
15 After receiving this revelation from God, my father was happy. He continued to praise God. His soul rejoiced and his heart was full of joy.
16 I (Nephi) do not write everything my father Lehi shared with our family. Lehi wrote many things he saw in visions and in dreams [in his own book]. He also wrote down many things he prophesied to his children. I will not write everything my father has already written.
17 I want to tell my story. However, before I do, I will continue to summarize my father’s story. Then I will return to my story.
18 After receiving these revelations, my father Lehi began to preach to the people. He declared what God showed him concerning the destruction of Jerusalem.
