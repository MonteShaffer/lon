2 Nephi 20
26 “God will incite a scourge for the Assyrian king. Yes, even like the slaughter of Midian at the rock of Oreb. Like when God raised His staff against the Egyptians at the Red Sea.”
27 “In that day, the Assyrian burden will be removed from your should. His yoke will be removed from your neck. Yes, it will removed by a King of Judah, anointed by God.”
28 “The Assyrian king enters Aiath. He passes through Migron. At Michmash, he reviews his weapons of war.”
29 “The army of Assyria has gone over the passage. It has taken up lodging at Geba. Ramah is afraid. Gibeah of Saul has fled.”
30 “Lift up your voice, O daughter of Gallim Raise the alarm to Laish, O poor Anathoth.”
31 “Those in Madmenah were chased away. The inhabitants of Gebim prepare themselves to flee.”
