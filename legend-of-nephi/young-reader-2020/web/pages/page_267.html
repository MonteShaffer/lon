2 Nephi 15
25 “Therefore, is God’s anger kindled against His people? Yes, God has stretch forth his hand against them. God has smitten them. And the hills tremble. Their carcasses were torn in the midst of the streets. For God’s anger is not turned away. God’s hand is stretched out still.”
26 “And God will lift up a banner to nations from afar. And God will whistle with a flute to all ends of the earth. Behold, they will swiftly respond and come speedily.”
27 “None will slumber nor sleep. None will be weary nor stumble. They will not loosen their belt. They will not loosen the strap of their sandals.”
28 “They will sharpen their arrows. They will bend their bows. Their horses’ hooves will be counted like flint. The wheels of (their chariots) will spin like a whirlwind and make a thunderous noise like a lion roaring.”
