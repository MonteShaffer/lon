1 Nephi 20
9 “Even so, I defer my anger. I will not cut you off. I will be patient from action. I do this to uphold my name. My name is my word. My word is my bond. It is honorable and of the highest rank.”
10 “I have refined you in your adversity. I have chose you in the furnace of affliction.”
11 “I do these things for My own purpose. I will not allow My name to be polluted. I will not give My glory and rank to another.”
12 “Listen to me O Jacob. Listen to me O Israel. I am He. (I am Jehovah). I am the first. I am also the last.”
13 “With Mine hand, I laid the foundation of the earth. With My right hand, I measure the heavens. When I call the host of heavens, they immediately answer Me by standing up together.”
