2 Nephi 26
9 “The Sun of Righteousness will appear to the faithful. He will heal them. They will have peace in Him. Yes, three generations will pass away in righteousness. And many of the fourth generation will also pass away in righteousness.”
10 “After this time of peace and righteousness, destruction will speedily come to my people. And it pains my soul, but I have seen it. I know it will happen. They will sell themselves for not. They will become proud and foolish. It will result in their destruction. They yield to the wicked one. They choose the works of darkness rather than light. Because of this, they must go down to hell.”
11 “For God’s Spirit will not always strive with man. When the God’s Spirit ceases to strive with man, speedily destruction comes. And this grieves my soul.”
