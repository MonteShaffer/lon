1 Nephi 4

1 I (Nephi) again responded to my brothers and their murmuring: “Let’s go back to Jerusalem again. Let’s be true and faithful to God in completing this task. God is mightier than Laban’s fifty. God is mightier than tens of thousands. God is mightier than all the earth.”
2 “Let’s go. Let’s be strong like Moses. Moses had faith and truly spoke to the waters of the Red Sea. And the waters parted so our fathers could cross on dry ground. When the armies of Pharaoh followed [on chariots], the waters crashed down upon them. They drowned.”
