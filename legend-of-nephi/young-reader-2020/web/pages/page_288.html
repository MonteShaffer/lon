2 Nephi 20
5 “(God speaking:) ” “O King of Assyria! You are the rod of My anger. In your hand I have placed the staff of My indignation.”
6 “And I will send this king against a hypocritical nation. In My wrath, I will allow this king to take spoils from this nation. Yes, to take captives from this nation. Even so, to subdue and defeat this nation.”
7 “Yet the king does not believe he is doing God’s will. In his heart, this is not the king’s intent. His intent is to destroy and subdue many nations.”
8 “As this king says: “Are not my princes like kings?”
9 “Is not Calno like Carchemish? Is not Hamath like Arpad? Is not Samaria like Damascus?”
10 “With my hand I have conquered all these kingdoms with their idols. Many of these local gods are mightier than the idols of Samaria and Jerusalem.”
