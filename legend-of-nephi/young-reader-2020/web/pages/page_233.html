2 Nephi 9
29 “To be learned is good. If you listen to God’s counsels.”
30 “Wo unto the rich. The rich as to things of this world. Because of their wealth, they despise the poor. They persecute the meek. They set their hearts on treasure. Treasure is their god. They will perish. And their treasure will perish with them.”
31 “Wo unto the deaf that will not hear God’s counsel. They will perish.”
32 “Wo unto the blind that will not see God’s goodness. They will perish.”
33 “Wo unto the uncircumcised of heart. In the last day, a knowledge of their iniquites will smite them.”
34 “Wo unto the liar. He will be thrust down to hell.’
35 “Wo unto the murderer. He that deliberately kills, he will die.”
36 “Wo unto those that commit whoredoms. They will be thrust down to hell.”
