1 Nephi 11
34 After He was slain, I saw multitudes of the earth. They gathered together to fight against the apostles of the Lamb. (The angel of the Lord called the twelve I saw in my vision apostles.)
35 I saw multitudes of the earth gather together. I saw that they were in a large and spacious building, like the building my father saw. The angel spoke again saying: “Behold the world and the wisdom thereof! Yes, the house of Israel has united with the world and its wisdom. Together, they gather to fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb.”
36 The great and spacious building represents the pride of the world. I saw it fall. I saw that its fall was exceedlingly great. I saw these things. I bear witness to what I saw. The angel again spoke to me saying: “Many will fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb. Nations, kindreds, tongues and people will fight them. And those that fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb will be destroyed. Their fall will be great.”
