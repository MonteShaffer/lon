1 Nephi 8
24 “Others were able to grab the end of the rod of iron. They did cling to the rod of iron. They pressed forward in the mist of darkness. They successfully reached the tree. They ate of the fruit.”
25 “After eating the fruit, [many] looked around as if they felt ashamed for eating it.”
26 “On the other side of the river, I saw a building. This building was great and spacious. It stood high above the earth. It stood as if it was in the air.”
27 “And the building was filled with people. Some were young. Some were old. Some were male. Some were female. They were all dressed with exceedingly fine apparel. They pointed their fingers at those who arrived at the tree. They pointed their fingers to mock those who ate of the fruit.”
