2 Nephi 22

1 “In that day, you will say in your heart:” “O God, I will praise You. Though You were angry with me, Your anger is no more. You comfort me.”
2 “Behold, God is my salvation. I will trust Him. I will not be afraid. For the Lord Jehovah has become my salvation. He is my strength. He is my song.”
3 “Therefore, with joy you will draw water out of the wells of salvation.”
4 “In that day, you will tell one another:” “Praise God. Call upon His name. Recognize His doings among the people. Remember that His name is exalted.”
5 “Praise God. He has done majestic things. The whole earth will recognize this.”
6 “Sing and shout, inhabitant of Zion. The perfect mighty power of the Eternal Father is among You! God prevails!”
