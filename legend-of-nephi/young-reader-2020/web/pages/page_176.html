2 Nephi 2
10 “The intercession is for all of the children of men. This means all men will stand before God and be judged by Him. They will be judged according to the truth and holiness which is in Him. They will be judged by the laws that Holy One has given. The ends of the law demand justice: inflicting punishment as the final judgment. Only the ends of the atonement can answer this demand: assigning happiness as the final judgment. Punishment and happiness are opposing judgments.”
