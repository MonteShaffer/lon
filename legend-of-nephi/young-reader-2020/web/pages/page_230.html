2 Nephi 9
19 “O the greatness of the mercy of our God, the Holy One of Israel. He delivers His saints from the awful monster the wicked one. He delivers them from death and hell. He delivers them from that lake of fire and brimstone. He delivers them from endless torment.”
20 “O how great the holiness of our God! He knows all things. There isn’t anything that He doesn’t know.”
21 “God comes to the world that He may save all men. If only they will listen to His voice. He suffers the pains of all men. Yes, He suffers the pains of every living creature. Both men and women. And children. Yes, everyone that belongs to the family of Adam.”
22 “God suffers this that the resurrection may pass to all men. That all might stand before Him at the great judgment day.”
