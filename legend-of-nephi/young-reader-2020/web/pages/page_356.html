2 Nephi 29
9 “And I do this to prove to many that I am the same. Yes, the same yesterday, today, and forever. And I speak My words as it pleases Me. Because I have spoken one word, you need not suppose that I cannot speak another. For My work is not yet finished. Neither will it be finished when the time of man ends. My work and glory is eternal. It will never end.”
10 “Wherefore, because you have a Bible, you need not suppose that it contains all My words. Neither should you suppose that I have not caused others to write more of My words.”
11 “For I command all men. Men in the east and men in the west. And in the north and in the south. And in the isles of the sea. I command all men to write the words which I speak. For out of these books which will be written I will judge the world. Every man will be judged according to his works, according to that which is written.”
