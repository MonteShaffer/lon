2 Nephi 6
8 “Now I (Jacob) want to explain these words. God has shown me what they mean. We have come from Jerusalem. Those that remained there have been killed or carried away as captives.”
9 “God has shown me that the captives will return again to Jerusalem. And God will manifest Himself to the house of Israel in the flesh. God, the Holy One of Israel, will be scourged by them. They will crucify their God (in the flesh). These are the words an angel shared with me.”
10 “After the house of Israel hardens their hearts. After they rejected the God in the flesh. God will send His judgments to them. They will be smitten and afflicted.”
