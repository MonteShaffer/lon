2 Nephi 13
6 “A man will supplicate (for his family) to any neighbor with a house and resources:” “You have clothing. Be our ruler. You have food. Please do not let us starve.”
7 “The neighbor will reply:” “I will not be your savior. For in my house, there is not enough bread nor clothing for you. I will not be your ruler.”
8 “For Jerusalem is ruined. Judah is fallen. They have spoken ill of their God. They have done things against God’s will. They rebel against the eyes of His glory.”
9 “Their outward appearence is a witness of their wickedness. Wo unto their souls. They have rewarded evil to themselves! They openly profess their sin to be even as Sodom. They do not hide it.”
10 “Say to the righteous that it is well with them. For they will eat the fruit of their works.”
