1 Nephi 7

1 After my father, Lehi, finished prophesying concerning his seed, God again spoke to Lehi. God said it was not proper for Lehi to take his family into the wilderness alone. Lehi’s sons would need wives so that they may raise up seed to God in the land of promise.
2 God gave us a new task. My brothers and I needed to return again to Jerusalem to invite Ishmael and his family to join us on this journey.
3 So again, my brothers and I journeyed to Jerusalem.
4 We traveled to the house of Ishmael. Ishmael received us with kindness. We shared with him what God had commanded.
5 God softened Ishmael’s heart. God softened the hearts of the members of Ishmael’s family. The entire household of Ishmael agreed to join us to go into the wilderness to the tent of my father.
