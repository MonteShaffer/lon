1 Nephi 16
14 We took our bows and arrows to kill wild animals as food for our families. We returned to our families at Shazer after a successful hunt. After a time, we again broke camp and continued our journey in the same direction. We were trying to keep to the more fertile parts of the wilderness. We were still in the region near the Red Sea.
15 We did travel for many days. We killed food along the way. We used our bows and arrows. We used our slings with stones.
16 We did follow the directions of the Liahona. We were led to the most fertile parts of the wilderness.
17 After traveling again for many days, we pitched our tents to rest. We agin went hunting to obtain food for our families.
18 I (Nephi) went out to hunt with the other men. During the outing, I broke my bow which was made of fine steel. It was the best bow our family had. The others got angry with me because I broke my bow. We did not have a successful hunt.
