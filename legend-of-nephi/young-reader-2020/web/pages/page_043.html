1 Nephi 8
18 “I beckoned to them, but they would not come to the tree.”
19 “Along the bank of the river, I saw a rod of iron. The rod of iron led to the tree.”
20 “I also saw a distressingly narrow path. This path ran adjacent to the rod of iron from the head of the river to the tree. The path continued into a large and spacious field. This field was so large, it was as if it was a world.”
21 “I saw uncountable assemblies of people in this field. Some were searching for a path that would lead them to the tree where I stood.”
22 “And many commenced on the path which led to the tree.”
23 “And a mist of darkness arose in the field. This mist create exceedingly great darkness. Many lost their way because of the darkness. They wandered off and were lost.”
