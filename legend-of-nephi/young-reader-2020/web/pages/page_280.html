2 Nephi 18
11 “For God has compelled me with a strong hand. God has instructed me that I should not agree with the designs of this people.”
12 “God instructed me to oppose the Assyrian confederacy. We should not fear what those that support the alliance fear. We should not be afraid.”
13 “Honor the Lord of Hosts Himself. Fear Him. Let Him be your dread.”
14 “God will be a sanctuary. He will be a stumbling stone for them. He will be a cliff from which they will fall. They being both the houses of Israel, the inhabitants of land of Jerusalem. He will trap them like birds. He will ensare them.”
15 “And many among them will stumble and fall. Many will be broken. Many will be ensnared. Many will be taken.”
16 “Bind up this testimony. Seal the instructions among my disciples.”
