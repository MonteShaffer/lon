2 Nephi 19
10 “The bricks are fallen down. But we will rebuild with hewn stone. The sycamores are cut down. But we will replant with cedars.”
11 “Therefore, God will exalt the princes of Resin against Ephraim. Yes, the princes will join together.”
12 “The Syrians before and the Philistines behind. They together will devour Israel with open mouth. God’s anger is not turned away. His hand is stretched out still.”
13 “The people did not turn to their God. Yes, their God that smites them. Neither do they enquire of Him.”
14 “Therefore, God will cut Israel from head to tail. In that day, God will cut down both the branch and the rush.”
15 “The Ancient who exalts his face is first (the head). The false prophet that establishes lies is last (the tail).”
16 “These leaders cause the people to err. The people that follow them are destroyed.”
