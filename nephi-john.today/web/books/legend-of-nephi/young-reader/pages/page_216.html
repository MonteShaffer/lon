2 Nephi 7
9 “For God will help me. If they condemn me, they will be cursed. They will wax old like a garment. The moth will eat them up.”
10 “Who among you fear God? Who obeys the voice of God’s servant? Good. Who walks is the darkness and has no light? Not good. You should walk in God’s light. You should rely on God.”
11 “Yet some will kindle their own fire. They will encircle themselves about with its sparks. They will walk in the light of their own fire. They will walk in the sparks which they kindled. God’s hand will deliver a judgment: they will lie down in sorrow.”
