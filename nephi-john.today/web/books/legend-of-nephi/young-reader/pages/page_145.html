1 Nephi 21

1 And again, the words of Isaiah: “Listen to me, remnant of the house of Israel. You are on isles far from me. You have been broken off from the house of Israel. You have been driven out of Jerusalem because of the wickedness of the Jewish leaders. You have been scattered abroad yet you are my people, remnant of the house of Israel. Yet listen to me and hear this. God has called me from the womb. From the womb of my mother God announced my name.”
2 “And God has made my tongue like a sharp sword. In the shelter of God’s hand I am concealed and protected. God has polished my messaging and delivery. Like an arrow I am. And God has a quiverful of arrows. In His quiver I am concealed and protected.”
3 “And God told me:” “You are my servant, son of Israel. In you, I will be glorified.”
