1 Nephi 5
10 After giving thanks to the God of Israel, my father, Lehi, turned his attention to the history engraven on the brass plates. He began to carefully study the contents, starting from the beginning.
11 Lehi learned that the record contained the five books of Moses. This includes an account of the creation of the world and the story of our first parents, Adam and Eve.
12 The record also contained the written history of the Jews. The history from the beginning to now. (At present, the current king of Judah is Zedekiah.)
13 The plates also contained the prophecies of the holy prophets. This includes prophets from the beginning to now. Many (not all) of Jeremiah’s prophecies are also written on the plates.
