2 Nephi 24
5 “God has removed the scepters from the kings. God has shattered the staff of the wicked one.”
6 “The wicked one who ruled the nations in anger. The wicked one who forced people into submission: to rebel with him or be killed. The wicked one is now persecuted. Nothing impedes God’s work.”
7 “Peace and quiet fill the earth. Inhabitants break the silence singing praises to God (zamar). They leap for joy!”
8 “Yes, the fir trees thank God (yadah). And the cedars of Lebanon thank God. They say: “Since the wicked one was put down, enemies no longer attack us. ”
9 “Hell is disturbed to see the wicked one enter therein. The imprisoned spirits are agitated. The seven guardians of Sheol pay attention. Yes, even the kings of the nations will rise from their thrones.”
