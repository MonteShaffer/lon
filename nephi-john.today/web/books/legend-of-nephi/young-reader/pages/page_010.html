1 Nephi 2

1 Thereafter, God spoke to my father Lehi in a dream and said: “You are blessed Lehi because you prophesied to the Jewish people as I asked. Because you have been faithful in preaching repentance to them, they want to kill you.”
2 In this dream, God commanded Lehi to take his family and leave the land of Jerusalem and go into the wilderness.
3 Lehi obeyed God and departed into the wilderness with his family.
4 Lehi left behind his house. He left behind the land of his inheritance. He left behind his gold, silver, and precious things. He took nothing with him but his family, provisions, and tents. He departed into the wilderness.
