2 Nephi 20
11 “I have conquered Samaria. Her idols did not protect her. Will I not also conquer Jerusalem? Do you think her idols will protect her?”
12 “One day, God will establish Mount Zion. God will restore Jerusalem. In that day, God will punish the fruit of the stout heart of the king of Assyria. Yes, the splendor of his haughty boastings will be answered.”
13 “For this king says:” “By the strength of my hand and by my wisdom I have done these things. For I am prudent. I have moved the borders of the people. I have robbed their treasures. I have subjected the inhabitants like a valiant man.”
14 “And my hand has found as a nest the riches of the people. I have gathered all the eggs of the earth. Not a bird moved its wing. Not a bird opened its mouth. Not a bird peeped.”
