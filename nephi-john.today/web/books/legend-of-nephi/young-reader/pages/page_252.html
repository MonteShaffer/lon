2 Nephi 12
9 “The natural man does not bow down. The great man will not humble himself. God will not forgive him.”
10 “O you wicked ones! Enter into the rock. Hide in the dust. For God’s punishment, in all His majesty and glory, will smite you!”
11 “Humanity will be humbled. The lofty looks of mighty man will be no more. The haughtiness of men will be bowed down. God alone will be exalted in that day.”
12 “For the day of the Lord of Hosts soon comes upon all nations. Yes, God comes to every one of them. Yes, to the proud and lofty. And to everyone who is lifted up. They will all be brought low.”
13 “Yes, the day of the Lord will come upon all the cedars of Lebanon. For they are high and lifted up. And upon all the oaks of Bashan.”
