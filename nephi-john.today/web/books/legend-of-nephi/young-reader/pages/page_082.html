1 Nephi 14
3 “The great and abominable church was founded by the wicked one and his followers to confound men. They want to led their souls to hell. Hell is a great pit dug by the wicked one and his followers to destroy men. (Not the everlasting destruction of the soul, but the casting down into hell that has no end.) One day, the pit (hell) will be filled by those who participated in digging it.”
4 “The wicked one ensnares men into the great pit of captivity. Hell is assigned according to the justice of God. All those who do wicked abominations before God will be cast into hell.”
5 Again, the angel spoke: “You have seen that if the Gentiles repent, it will be well with them. You also know about God’s covenants with the house of Israel. If one repents, he will not perish.”
6 “Therefore, wo unto the Gentiles if they harden their hearts against the Lamb of God!”
