2 Nephi 19

2 Nephi 19

1 “This darkness will not be like before. Like when those in the land of Zebulun and Naphtali were afflicted. This will be worse. By the way of sea. From beyond Jordan. In Galilee of the nations.”
2 “The people that walked in darkness will see a great light. Yes, those that dwell in the land of the shadow of death. This great light will shine upon them.”
3 “God has magnified the nation. He has given the nation joy. They rejoice in the harvest. They rejoice like men do when they divide the spoil.”
4 “The people has broken the yoke of the oppressor’s burden. They have broken the staff of the oppressor’s shoulder. They have broken the rod of the oppressor.”
