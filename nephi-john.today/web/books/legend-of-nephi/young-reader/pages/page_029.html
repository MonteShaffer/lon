1 Nephi 5
5 “God has granted us a better land of inheritance, a land of promise. I am happy for this. I am certain that God will deliver our sons out of the hands of Laban. I know God will bring them back to us in the wilderness.”
6 In this manner, my father, Lehi, tried to comfort my mother Sariah while we were gone.
7 When we arrived, my father was filled with joy. My mother was comforted.
8 My mother said: “Now I know for sure that God commanded my husband to flee Jerusalem and come into the wilderness. I also know for certain that God protected my sons. God delivered them out of the hands of Laban. God gave them power to accomplish the task God had given them.”
9 My parents rejoiced exceedingly when we returned. They offered a sacrifice and burnt offerings to God. They gave thanks to the God of Israel.
