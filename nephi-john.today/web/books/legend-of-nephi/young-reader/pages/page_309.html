2 Nephi 24
25 “I will break Assyria. In My lands. On My mountains. I will trample him to pieces. My people will be freed from the burden of his oppression.”
26 “This is the purpose of the earth since the beginning. To all nations, My hand is stretched out still.”
27 “If God has set this purpose, who can frustrate it? And if God’s hand is stretched out still, who can challenge it?”
28 “This prophecy (oracle) was given in the year king Ahaz died.”
29 “Palestine, you think you can rejoice because the rod of him that smote you is broken. Think again. A nachash has children. From this nachash, a basilisk will emerge. And from this progeny, a flying seraph will emerge.”
30 “The firstborn of the poor will now have food to spare. The oppressed will now lie down in safety. God will kill the seed of the wicked one with famine. God will kill all remnants of the seed of the wicked one.”
