2 Nephi 9
14 “Our knowledge will be perfect. We will know of all of our guilt. We will know of our uncleanness. We will know of our nakedness. The righteous will have a perfect knowledge of their righteousness. They will enjoy it. They will be clothed with purity. They will wear the robes of righteousness.”
15 “All men will pass from this first (temporal) death to life. Once they become immortal, they will appear before God’s judgment seat. The Holy One of Israel will judge them. They will be judged according to God’s holy judgment.”
