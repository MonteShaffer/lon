2 Nephi 31
8 “Wherefore, after the Lamb of God was baptized by water, the Holy Ghost descended upon Him in the form of a dove.”
9 “The Lamb of God set an example before the children of men. This example showed them the narrowness of the gate they need to enter. This example showed them the straitness of the path they need to follow.”
10 “And He declares to the children of men:” “Come follow me.” ‘Wherefore, my beloved brothers, how can we follow Yeshua? To follow Yeshua, we need to be willing to keep the Father’s commandments.”
11 “For the Father commands:” “Repent! Repent! Be baptized in the name of my Beloved Son.”
12 “And the voice of the Son declares:” “He that is baptized in My name, to him will the Father give the Holy Ghost. As the Father has given Me the Holy Ghost. Wherefore, follow Me. Do the things which you have seen Me do.”
