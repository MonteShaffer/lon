1 Nephi 17
14 God had also said: “After you arrive in the promised land, you will know that I am Lord God. That I did deliver you from destruction. That I did bring you out of the land of Jerusalem.”
15 I strived to be faithful and diligent in keeping God’s commandments. I also exhorted my brothers to do the same.
16 And I did make tools from the ore. I extracted this melted ore from the rock.
17 And when my brothers figured out what I was doing, the murmuring commenced: “Our brother is a fool. He thinks he can build a ship. He thinks he can cross these many waters.”
18 My brothers complained because they did not want to help. It was hard work. They thought it was a waste of time and effort. They didn’t believe I was capable of building a ship. They didn’t believe God gave me instructions on how to build the ship.
