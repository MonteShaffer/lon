2 Nephi 26
29 “God has commanded that there will be no priestcrafts. A priestcraft is when a man preaches and sets himself up to be the light of the world. They do so to get gain and praise of the world. They do not seek to support the cause of Zion.”
30 “Behold, God does not like this. God commands all to love humanity. To love the name of Jehovah is to serve His children. Men are nothing without Jehovah’s love. Wherefore, if they understand this, they will not allow any laborer in Zion to fail.”
31 “The laborer in Zion will labor for Zion. If they labor for other reasons (such as money), they will fail.”
