1 Nephi 20
20 “But a remnant will I redeem to fulfill promises made to my servant Jacob. This remnant will be guided to escape the might of Babylon. This remnant will flee from the Chaldeans before Jerusalem is destroyed.” “This remnant will sing praises to God for being delivered which will spread to the ends of the earth:” “God has redeemed His servant Jacob!”
21 “As they traveled, the remnant did not thirst. God led them through the deserts. God caused water to flow out of the rock for them. God also cleaved the rock. Waters gushed out.”
22 “God had done all this. God had even done greater things. Even so, some continue in unrighteousness. As God says:” “Those who deviate from My law will not have peace of mind.”
