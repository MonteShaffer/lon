2 Nephi 10
8 “They will be gathered together after their long dispersion. They will be gathered from the isles of the sea. They will be gathered from the four parts of the earth. And the Gentiles will be great in my eyes. The Gentiles will carry them to the lands of their inheritance.”
9 “Yes, the kings of the Gentiles will be nourishing fathers for them. The queens of the Gentiles will become their nursing mothers. God’s promises are great even unto the Gentiles. For God has spoken it, and who will dispute this?”
10 “God has said that this land will be a land of your inheritance. The Gentiles will also be blessed on this land.”
11 “This land will be a land of liberty for the Gentiles. The Gentiles will not raise up any kings on this land.”
12 “God promises:” “I will fortify this land against all other nations.”
13 “And he that fights against Zion will perish.”
