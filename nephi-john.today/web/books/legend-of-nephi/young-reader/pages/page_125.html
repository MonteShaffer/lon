1 Nephi 18
10 I spoke to them with soberness. I was afraid God would become angry with us. God may see this behavior and destroy us. God may have the sea swallow our ship into the depths. They did not want to hear my rebuke. They got angry, saying: “Our younger brother will not tell us what to do. He is not the ruler over us.”
11 Laman and Lemuel did take hold of me. They did bind me with cords. They treated me harshly. God allowed me to suffer for a time. God wanted to show His power. God wanted to teach them a lesson.
12 My brothers had bound me with cords. I could not move. The Liahona stopped working.
13 My brothers did not know which direction to steer the ship. Then a great storm arose. Violent winds drove the ship back in the direction from which we came. For three days the storm raged. Finally, my brothers became frightened. They were afraid they would drown in the sea. Yet they would not free me from my bonds.
