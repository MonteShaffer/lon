1 Nephi 12
20 Indeed, I saw the seed of my brothers overpower the people of my seed. In fact, they destroyed my seed. Thereafter, the seed of my brothers went forth in multitudes upon the face of this promised land.
21 I saw them gather together in multitudes. Among them, I saw wars and rumors of wars. This tradition of warring continued for many generations.
22 And the angel spoke: “Behold the seed of your brothers have dwindled in unbelief!”
23 And I saw that this was true. They had dwindled in unbelief. They had become dark. They had become loathsome. They had become a filthy people. There were full of idleness. They did all manner of abominations.
