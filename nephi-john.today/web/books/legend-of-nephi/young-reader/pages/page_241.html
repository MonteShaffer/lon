2 Nephi 10
4 “If He were perform mighty miracles dwelling among any other people. This other people would recognize Him as God. They would repent.”
5 ‘But not the Jews. Because of their priestcrafts. Because of their iniquities. They at Jerusalem will stiffen their necks against Him. They will crucify Him.”
6 “They will be visited with destructions, famines, and pestilences. Bloodshed will come upon them. Those not destroyed will be scattered among all nations.”
7 “As God has said:” “When the day comes. The day they will believe in Me. The day they will believe that I am the Messiah. I covenanted with their fathers that they will be restored. In the flesh, on this earth, they will be restored to the lands of their inheritance.”
