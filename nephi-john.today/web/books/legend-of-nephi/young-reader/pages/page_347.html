2 Nephi 28
12 “Their churches have become corrupted. Because of pride. Because of false teachers. Because of false doctrine. Their churches are lifted up. Because of pride their leaders are puffed up.”
13 “They rob the poor to build their finest temples, tabernacles, and worship centers. They rob the poor to buy the highest-quality clothing. They persecute the meek because of their pride. They persecute the poor in heart because they are puffed up in their hearts.”
14 “They wear stiff necks and high heads. Yes, almost all have gone astray. Because of pride. Because of wickedness. Because of abominations. Because of whoredoms. Only a few humble followers of the Messiah remain. But they still err. Because of those who lead them. They are being taught the philosophies of men mingled with scripture.”
