2 Nephi 17
15 “This son will eat cheese and honey. He will refuse the evil portion. He will choose the good part.”
16 “Yes, this child will know to refuse the evil. He will know to choose the good---And the land of your enemies will be forsaken of both her kings.”
17 “God will deliver you from the current enemy. But new days of oppression will come. For you. For your people. For your father’s house. Days worse than the time Ephraim revolted and departed from Judah. The kingdom of Assyria will be worse.”
18 “In that day, Egypt will gather her armies on the Nile (numerous as flies). And Assyria will gather her armies (numerous as bees). And God will call them to battle each other.”
19 “And they will battle here in the land of Judah. They will fill the land. Yes, the valleys. The caves. The trees and bushes.”
