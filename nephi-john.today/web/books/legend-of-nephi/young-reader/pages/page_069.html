1 Nephi 13

1 The angel spoke: “Look!” And I looked. I saw many nations. I saw many kingdoms.
2 The angel asked: “What do you see?” And I answered: “I see many nations. I see many kingdoms.”
3 The angel responded: “Yes. These are the nations and kingdoms of the Gentiles.”
4 I saw that a great church was formed. I saw that it was formed among the nations of the Gentiles.
5 And the angel spoke: “Behold the formation of an abominable church! This church is the most abominable of all other churches. This church kills the saints of God. This church tortures them. This church oppresses them. This church yokes them with chains. This church enslaves them.”
6 I saw this great and abominable church. I saw that the church was founded by the wicked one.
