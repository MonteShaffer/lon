1 Nephi 3
24 We went into Laban’s house and again requested that he give us the family history engraven on the brass plates. In exchange, we would give him all of this gold, silver, and precious things.
25 When Laban saw our substantial riches, he wanted them. He took them by force. He told us to get out and commanded his servants to kill us. He did this so he could take our property.
26 We did run away from Laban’s servants. As a result, we left behind our property. Laban seized it for his own.
27 We ran into the wilderness. We hid in the hollow of a rock. Laban’s servants did not find us.
28 Laman was angry with me. Laman was also angry with my father. Lemuel agreed with Laman, and was also very angry. Together they spoke harshly to Sam and I. They were so angry that they began to strike us with a stick.
