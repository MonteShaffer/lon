2 Nephi 17
4 “And say unto him: ‘Restrain yourself and be at peace. Fear not, neither be faint-hearted.’ Yes, the fierce anger of Rezin of Syria and the son of Remaliah are against you. But they are like two tails of smoking firebrands.
5 “Syria and Ephraim (the son of Remaliah) have taking evil counsel against you saying:”
6 “Let us go up against Judah. Let us create havoc and fear. Let us breach the walls of the city. Let us place the son of Tabeal as the king of the city.”
7 “But God has spoken:” “This plan will not be fulfilled.”
8 “The capital of Syria is Damascus. And Rezin is the king of Damascus.”
