2 Nephi 12
20 “In that day, a man will throw out his idols. His idols of silver. His idols of Gold. Idols which he made for himself to worship. He will throw them out to the moles and the bats.”
21 “And the wicked will go into the clefts of the rocks. And into the tops of the ragged rocks. For God’s punishment, in all His majesty and glory, will smite you! The earth will shake terribly.”
22 “Don’t subject yourself to a man and his kingdom. Today he lives, tomorrow he dies. Trusting in man has no value. Only God can save you from destruction.”
