2 Nephi 2
18 “Because the wicked one had fallen, he had become miserable forever. This is why the wicked one wants all mankind to be miserable. The wicked one is the fleeing nachash (Lucifuge). The wicked one is the father of all lies. The wicked one said to Eve:” “Partake of the fruit of the tree of knowledge found here in the middle of the garden. You will not die. You will become like God, knowing good and evil.”
19 “Adam and Eve partook of this fruit. And God had to cast them out of the Garden of Eden. They had to till the earth.”
20 “And Adam and Even brought forth children. Yes, even the family of all the earth.”
21 “And the days of the children of men were prolonged. This was agreeable to God’s will. This state became a state of probation. Their time was lengthened. This is according to the commandments God gave to the children of men. God commanded all men to repent. God showed all men that they were lost. They were lost because of the transgression of their parents.”
