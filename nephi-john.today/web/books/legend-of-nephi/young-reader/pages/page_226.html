2 Nephi 9
9 “Our spirits would have become like his. We would have become servants to the wicked one. We would be shut out from God’s presence. We would remain with the father of lies (the wicked one). We would be miserable as he is miserable. The wicked one that misled our first parents. The wicked one that transforms himself to appear to be an angel of light. The wicked one that deceives the children of men. The wicked one that convinces them to murder and perform all manner of secret works of darkness. The wicked one that entices them to make evil oaths, even secret combinations.”
10 “O how great the goodness of our God. God prepared a way for our escape from the grasp of this awful monster. Yes, that monster is death (of the body). That monster is hell (death of the spirit).”
11 “God will deliver us. The grave will deliver up its dead. The temporal death will be overcome. It will be overcome by the Holy One of Israel.”
