1 Nephi 3
7 I (Nephi) replied to my father: “I will go and do the things God commands. I know that God doesn’t give a task to His children unless He has prepared a way for them to fulfill the task.”
8 When Lehi heard my response, he was very happy. He knew that God had touched my heart.
9 So I (Nephi) and my brothers began our journey in the wilderness, with our tents, to return to the land of Jerusalem.
10 When we returned to Jerusalem, we consulted with each other to determine how we should accomplish this task.
11 We cast lots to determine who should go to Laban’s house. The lot fell upon Laman, so Laman went to Laban’s house. Laman sat with Laban in his house.
12 Laman shared his desire to get the family history from Laban.
