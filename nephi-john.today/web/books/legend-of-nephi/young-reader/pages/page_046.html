1 Nephi 8
34 Lehi said: “If a person ate the fruit, and was ashamed, they would fall away onto strange roads and be lost.”
35 Lehi also said: “Laman and Lemuel did not eat of the fruit.”
36 My father described in great detail the many things he saw in his vision. After, he said: “I fear for you Laman and Lemuel. This vision worries me. I exceedingly fear that you will be cast off from the presence of God.”
37 Lehi exhorted them. He wanted them to listen to his words. He wanted God to have mercy on them. He didn’t want God to cast them out. With genuine feelings of a caring parent, Lehi preached to them.
38 After he preached to them, he prophesied to them many things. He asked them to keep God’s commandments. [Finally,] he stopped speaking.
