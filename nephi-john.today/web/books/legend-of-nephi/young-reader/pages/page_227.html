2 Nephi 9
12 “On the one side, spirit prison must also deliver up its dead. Spiritual death is hell. And hell will deliver up its dead. Hell will deliver up its captive spirits. The grave will deliver up its captive bodies. The bodies and the spirits of men will be again restored one to the other. This is possible through the power of the resurrection of the Holy One of Israel.”
13 “O how great the plan of our God! On the other side, God’s spirit paradise must deliver up the righteous spirits. The grave will deliver up the righteous bodies. And the spirit and body will be restored again one to the other. And the flesh all men will become incorruptible and immortal. They will be living souls. They will have their senses like they did in the flesh, but the veil will be removed. Their new awareness will be perfect.”
