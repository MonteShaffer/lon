2 Nephi 31
4 Wherefore, do you remember that one prophet I told you about? A prophet that God showed me. “This prophet would baptize the Messiah. Yes, even the Lamb of God. He who will take away the sins of the world.”
5 “Now, the Lamb of God was holy. Yet He was baptized by water. He did so to fulfill all righteousness. We are not holy. O then, how much more do we need to be baptized. Yes, even by water!”
6 “Now, I ask you, my beloved brothers. How did the Lamb of God fulfill all righteousness by being baptized by water?”
7 “Don’t you know He was holy? Even though He was holy, He was still baptized by water. He showed the children of men an example. Even He, in the flesh, would humble Himself before God the Eternal Father. Even He would witness to the Eternal Father that He would obey the Father and keep the Father’s ways.”
