2 Nephi 13
17 “God will smite her. The beautiful daughter of Zion will be exposed to sickness that will make her bald. God will strip away her fine apparel. In shame, her secret parts will be exposed.”
18 “In that day, God will take away her bravery. He will take away her tinkling ornaments (anklets). He will take away her head dressings. He will take away her body ornaments shaped like the moon.”
19 “Her chains and the bracelets. Her long-flowing veils.”
20 “Her bonnets. The ornaments of her legs. Her headbands. Her perfume bottles. Her ear-rings.”
21 “Her rings. Her nose jewels.”
22 “Her changeable suits of apparel. Her tunics. Her shawls. Her purses.”
23 “Her vanity mirrors. Her fine linen undergarments. Her headscarves. Her veils.”
