1 Nephi 21
4 “And I responded to God:” “(How will You be glorified?) I have failed you. I have labored in vain. My efforts have been worthless. Yet, I tried. I will respect Your judgment. You will determine my reward.”
5 “And God answered me. The God that formed me from the womb to be His servant. The God who tasked me with bringing Jacob’s house back to Him. He said:” “Yes, you tried. Although Israel has not returned to Me, you will be glorified for your work. You are mine because you have labored for Me.”
6 “You have been a light for the world. You have roused the tribes of Jacob to repent. You have brought the remnant of the House of Israel to a restored knowledge of their purpose. You will also be a light for the world for the Gentiles. Through you, the ends of the earth will know that salvation is only possible through Me.”
