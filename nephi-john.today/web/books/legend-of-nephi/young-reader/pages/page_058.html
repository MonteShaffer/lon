1 Nephi 11
16 And the angel asked: “Do you understand the condescension of God?”
17 I answered: “I know that He loves His children. Nevertheless, I don’t know the meaning of all things.”
18 The angel said: “Behold the virgin you see is the mother of the Son of God, after the manner of the flesh.”
19 I saw that she was carried away in the Spirit. Some time thereafter the angel said: “Look!’
20 And I looked. I saw the virgin again. She was carrying a child in her arms.
21 And the angel said: “This is the Lamb of God. He is the Son of the Eternal Father!” The angel then asked: “Do you understand the meaning of the tree which your father did see?”
