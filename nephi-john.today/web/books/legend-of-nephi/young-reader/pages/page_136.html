1 Nephi 19
16 “God will also remember the covenants He made to those on the isles of the sea” said the prophet Zenos. “He will gather all who are of the house of Israel. God says: “I will gather my people from the four quarters of the earth.”
17 ‘Yes, all the earth will see the salvation of God” said the prophet. “Every nation, kindred, tongue, and people will be blessed when salvation comes.”
18 I (Nephi) write these things for my people. Perhaps, I might persuade them to remember God as their Messiah, Savior, and Redeemer.
19 I speak to all of the house of Israel. One day, they will obtain my words.
