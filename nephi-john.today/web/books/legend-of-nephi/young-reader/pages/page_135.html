1 Nephi 19
13 “And those in Jerusalem who crucified God,” said the prophet, “They will be severely punished by all nations for what they have done. They have crucified the God of Israel. They have rejected signs and wonders. They have turned their hearts from the power and glory of the God of Israel.”
14 “And because they turned their hearts,” said the prophet, “They will be hated of all nations. They have despised the Holy One of Israel. As a result, they will wander in the flesh, be scattered and destroyed. Their name Jews will be commonly spoken with contempt and disapproval.”
15 “Neverless,” said the prophet, “One day they will repent. They will turn their hearts again to the Holy One of Israel. God will then honor the covenants He has made with their forefathers.”
