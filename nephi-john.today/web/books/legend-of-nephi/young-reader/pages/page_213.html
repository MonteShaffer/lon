2 Nephi 6
16 (From Isaiah): “Will the prey be taken from the mighty? Will the lawful captives be delivered?”
17 “God has promised:” “Even the captives of the mighty will will be freed. Even the prey of the terrible will be delivered.” “The Mighty God will deliver His covenant people. God has promised:” “I will content with those that contend with you.”
18 “And I will feed those that oppress you with their own flesh. They will be made drunk with their own blood. As if it were sweet wine. All flesh will know that I am God. I am the Savior and the Redeemer. I am the Mighty One of Jacob.”
