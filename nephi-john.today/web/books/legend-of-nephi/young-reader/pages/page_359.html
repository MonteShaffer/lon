2 Nephi 30
3 Now, I want to prophesy a bit more about the Jews and the Gentiles. Recall that this book will come forth. It will be written to the Gentiles. It will be sealed up again to God. At that time, many Gentiles will believe the words written. And they will carry the words to the remnant of our seed.
4 “In that day, the remnant of our seed will know. They will know about us. They will know how we came out of Jerusalem. They will know they are descendants of the Jews.”
5 “And the gospel of Yeshua the Annointed One will be declared to them. Wherefore, they will be restored to the knowledge of their fathers. They will be restored to the knowledge of Yeshua as the Eternal Father’s Emissary. Yes, a knowledge their fathers had possessed.”
