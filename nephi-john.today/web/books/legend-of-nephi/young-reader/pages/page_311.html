2 Nephi 25

1 Now, I (Nephi) speak about the words I have written. Yes, the words Isaiah spoke. Behold, many of my people find these things hard to understand. For they do not know the manner in which the Jews prophesy.
2 For I have not taught my people many things about the manner of the Jews. For many of their works were works of darkness. For many of their doings were doings of abominations.
3 Wherefore, I write to my people. Even to all those that will receive these things after I write them. That they may know God’s judgments. God’s judgments will come to all nations according to His word.
