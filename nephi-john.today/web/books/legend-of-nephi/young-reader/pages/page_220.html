2 Nephi 8
15 “I am the Lord, your God! I divided the sea and the waves roared. The Lord of Hosts is My name.”
16 “I put My words in your mouth. I have covered you in the shadow of My hand. I will plant a new heaven. I will lay foundations for a new earth. I will say to Zion: Behold, you are my people.”
17 “Wake up! Wake up! Stand up, O Jerusalem! You have drunk from the cup of God’s fury. You are drunk from the dregs of this terrible cup. You have drunk every last drop.”
18 “There are none to guide you in this state. None among all the sons you brought forth. Of all your sons, none take you by the hand.”
19 “These two sons come to you. Who will feel sorry for you. Your desolation. Your destruction. The famine and the sword. By whom will I comfort you?”
