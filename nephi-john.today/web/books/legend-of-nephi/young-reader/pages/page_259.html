2 Nephi 13
24 “In that day. Instead of sweet smell, she will be stink. Instead of a belt, a cord of a slave. Instead of braided hair, baldness. Instead of garment worked with purple to support her breasts, she will wrap herself in sackcloth. Instead of beauty, she will be branded with fire.”
25 “Her men will fall by the sword. Her mighty will die in war.”
26 “Zion will lament and mourn. Her gates will be open and desolate. Zion will be made low. Her haughtiness is no more.”
