1 Nephi 14
17 “And the wrath of God will be poured out upon the mother of harlots, the great and abominable church of all the earth. The church founded by the wicked one. When wrath comes, the work of the Father will begin. The way will be prepared for God to fulfill all covenants he promised to His people (the house of Israel).”
18 And the angel said: “Look!”
19 And I looked. I saw a man dressed in a white robe.
20 And the angel said: “Behold one of the twelve apostles of the Lamb of God!”
21 “This apostle will see and write the remainder of these things that are to come. He will also see and write many things which have already happened.”
22 “And he will write about the end of the world.”
