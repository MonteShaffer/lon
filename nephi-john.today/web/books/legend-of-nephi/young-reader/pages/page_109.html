1 Nephi 17
4 We did sojourn for many years. In total, we journeyed for eight years in the wild.
5 We arrived at a land which we named Bountiful. We named the place Bountiful because it was bountiful with fruit and wild honey. God prepared this place for us. We saw a great sea. We called this sea Irreantum which means many waters.
6 We pitched our tents by the seashore. We had suffered tremendously on our journey. So much suffering that we cannot write it all down. Even so, we rejoiced exceedingly when we arrived at this seashore. We called the place Bountiful because it had so much fruit.
7 I rested in the land of Bountiful for many days. God’s voice called to me: “Arise! Go to that mountain!” And I arose. I ascended to the mountain. And I cried to God.
8 In the mountain, God instructed me: “You need to build a ship. Let me show you how to build the ship. [Nephi is shown.] With this ship, I will carry your people across these [many] waters.”
