2 Nephi 6

1 These are the words of Jacob, the brother of Nephi. This is what Jacob said to the people of Nephi:
2 “Behold, my beloved brothers. I (Jacob) have been called by God. I have been ordained after the manner of His hold order. My brother Nephi has consecrated me. The same Nephi you look to as your king or protector. The same Nephi you depend on for your safety. And you know that I have told you exceedingly many things.”
3 “Nevertheless, I speak unto you again. I am concerned for the welfare of your souls. I have great anxiety because of you. And you are aware of this. I have exhorted you diligently. I have taught you the words of my father. I have spoken to you concerning everything that has been written. I have taught you many things starting with the creation of the world.”
