2 Nephi 33

1 And now I (Nephi) cannot write all the things which were taught my people. Neither am I mighty in writing. I am better at speaking. When a man speaks by the power of the Holy Ghost. The power of the Holy Ghost carries the message to the hearts of the children of men.
2 But behold, there are many that harden their hearts against the Holy Spirit. It has no place in them. Wherefore, they throw away the things written. They do not value them.
3 “Nevertheless, I have written what I have written. I esteem them to have great value. Especially for my people. I pray for them continual by day. And my eyes water my pillow by night. Because of the, I cry unto my God in faith. And I know that God will hear my cry.”
