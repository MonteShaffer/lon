2 Nephi 1
19 “O my sons, I pray these things don’t happen to you. I pray you might be a choice and favored people. Behold, God’s will be done. God’s ways are righteous.”
20 “And God has said:” “If you keep my commandments, you will prosper in the land. If you do not keep my commandments, you will be cut off from my presence.”
21 “I wish my soul might have joy in you, my sons. I wish I might leave this world glad in heart because of you, my sons. I wish that I might not be brought to grief and sorrow to the grave. Therefore, Arise from the dust my sons and be men! Be determined in one mind and in one heart. Be united in all things. That you may not come down into captivity.”
22 “That you may not be cursed with a sore cursing. That you may not incur the displeasure of a just God upon you. That you won’t be destroyed. Yes, destroyed in both the soul and the body!”
