2 Nephi 15
14 “Therefore, hell has enlarged herself. Hell has opened her mouth without measure. And many will descend into hell. Those that rejoice in their own glory. In their own multitude. In their own pomp.”
15 “The natural man will bow down. The champion will be humbled. The eyes of the lofty will sink.”
16 “[They will bow before] God. He that is the Lord of Hosts. He will be exalted in judgment. He that is Holy. He will be sanctified in righteousness.”
17 “The property of the haughty will be desolate. Lambs will graze in these places. Yes, lambs owned by strangers.”
18 “Wo unto those the gratify iniquity with snares of vanity. Those that sin as it were with a cart rope.”
19 “Those that say:” “Let God make speed. He should hurry up so we can see His work. Let the purposes of the Holy One of Israel speedily come. That we may know them.”
