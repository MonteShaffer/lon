2 Nephi 20
20 “In that day, those who escaped of Jacob’s house will no longer depend on Assyria. The remnant of Israel will turn to the true and living God.”
21 “Yes, the remnant of Jacob will return. Will return to the valiant God.”
22 “The people of Israel had been as the sand of the sea. God’ wrath pruned this number to a small remnant that will return. And this remnant will overflow with righteousness.”
23 “Yes, God’s wrath will prune all those throughout the land.”
24 “And God will declare:” “O my people that dwell in Zion. Be not afraid of Assyria. This king will smite you with a rod. He will lift his staff against you, after the manner of Egypt.”
25 “After a short time, My indignation will end. Yes, My anger of destruction will subside.”
