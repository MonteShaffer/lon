1 Nephi 17
31 “God’s word is powerful. By His word, He can destroy them. By His word, He can lead them. By His word, He can do all things for them. And everything that was done, was done by His word.”
32 “After the children of Israel crossed the river Jordan, God made them mighty. He gave them the power to drive out the children of the land. He gave the children of Israel the power to scatter and destroy the children of the land.”
33 “And what of these children of the land? Why were they driven out of the land of promise and destroyed? Do you suppose they were righteous? I tell you, No!”
34 “If the children of the land had been righteous, would God have made our fathers more choice? I tell you, No!”
