1 Nephi 4
14 After hearing these words, I also remembered what God had taught me when I prayed in the wilderness: “If your seed keeps My commandments, they will prosper in the promised land.”
15 I reasoned that my posterity could not keep God’s commandments (according to the law of Moses) if they did not have a written record of the law.
16 I knew the law was engraven on the brass plates.
17 I also knew that God had delivered Laban into my hands. God wanted me to complete this task and get the family genealogy and the written record of the prophets.
18 Finally, I obeyed the voice of the Spirit. I took Laban by the hair of his head. I cut off his head with his own sword.
19 After killing Laban with his own sword, I put on Laban’s clothing. I even put on his armor.
