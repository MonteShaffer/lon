1 Nephi 21
7 “And God continued to instruct me. The God who is the Redeemer of Israel. The God who is the Holy One. Me whom man will despise. Me whom nations will abhore. Me whom will be a servant to rulers. God said:” “Kings will turn to Me and increase in power. Their sons (princes) will worship Me. All because you have been faithful advising them for Me.”
8 “And God said:” “In due time, I will hear you, O isles of the sea. In a day of salvation, I will help you. I will preserve you that you may reclaim your deserted inheritances. I will give you another servant to teach you about Me. I will make the earth firm for you. I will ratify the covenant I have made to My People.”
