2 Nephi 8
20 “All your sons have fainted. They lie dead at the end of every street. Except for these two sons. These two are raging like wild bulls caught in a trap. They are full of God’s fury. They rebuke the mighty ones.”
21 “Hear now this. You that are afflicted. You that are drunk, but not drunk from wine:”
22 “Thus says your God. Your God that pleads for the cause of His people:” “Behold, I have taken the cup out of your trembling hand. The cup that contains the dregs of My fury. You will not have to drink from it again.”
23 “But I will put the cup in the hand of those that afflict you. Those who commanded your soul:” “Bow down, that we may go over.” “Those who would walk over you. Those that laid your body on the ground of the street. Those would walk over you.”
