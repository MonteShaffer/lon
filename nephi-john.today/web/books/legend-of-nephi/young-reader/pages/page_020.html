1 Nephi 3
29 God sent an angel to stop this physical assault: “Why do you strike your younger brother with this stick? Don’t you know that he will rule over you because of your improper behavior? Go back to Jerusalem, and God will deliver Laban into your hands.”
30 After the angel said these things, he departed.
31 After the angel departed, Laman and Lemuel again murmured saying: “How will God deliver Laban into our hands? Laban is a mighty man. He can command fifty. He can even slay fifty. Why won’t he slay the four of us!”
