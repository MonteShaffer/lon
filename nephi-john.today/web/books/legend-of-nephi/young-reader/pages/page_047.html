1 Nephi 9

1 All of these things happened as my father Lehi dwelt in a tent in the valley of Lemuel. Lehi saw, heard, and said much. I can’t write them all on these plates. There is not enough space.
2 These small plates do not contain a full account of the history of my people. I have created other plates that have the full account. I call them the plates of Nephi. These small plates I also call the plates of Nephi.
3 God commanded me that these small plates have a special purpose. The purpose is the provide a spiritual account of the ministry of my people.
