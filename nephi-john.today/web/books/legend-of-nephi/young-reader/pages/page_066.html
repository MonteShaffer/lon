1 Nephi 12
12 I (Nephi) saw the fourth generation. Many of this generation also passed away in righteousness.
13 I saw that the multitudes gathered together again.
14 And the angel spoke: “Behold your seed! Behold the seed of your brothers!”
15 And I looked. I saw the people of my seed gathered together in multitudes against the seed of my brothers. They were gathered for battle.
16 And the angel spoke: “Behold the fountain of filthy water which your father saw! This river of filth is deep. Indeed, the depths of this river are the depths of hell.”
17 “And the mists of darkness are the temptations of the wicked one. These mists blind a man’s eyes. These mists harden a man’s heart. These mists lead a man away onto strange roads. Because of these mists, many of the children of men perish and are lost.”
