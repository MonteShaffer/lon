2 Nephi 27
25 “This people draws near Me with their words. With their lips they do honor Me. But they have distanced their hearts from Me. According to man’s precepts they teach a false reverence. This is not correct.”
26 “So, I will do this marvelous work for this people. Even a marvelous work and a wonder. For the wisdom of the wily will be destroyed. And distinguishing discernment will be absent.”
27 “Wo unto those that conceal their profound skill from God! A deed in the darkness ! They ask themselves:” “How can we be seen? How can we be renowned? Surely, we can perversely convince them that our philosophies are really the potter’s clay.” “And God will answer:” “I will show them that I know what they are doing. The work I created tells Me, the creator, that I did not make them?! Yes, the frame I created tells Me, the creator, that I did not form them?!”
