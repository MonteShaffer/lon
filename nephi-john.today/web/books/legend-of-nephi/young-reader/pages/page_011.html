1 Nephi 2
5 Lehi and his family traveled in the wilderness near the shore of the Red Sea. Then, they traveled nearer the shore of the Red Sea. Lehi traveled with his family: my mother Sariah and my elder brothers Laman, Lemuel, and Sam [and me Nephi of course].
6 After traveling for [an additional] three days in the wilderness, Lehi pitched his tent in a valley along the shore of a river.
7 Lehi built an altar of stones and made an offering to God. Lehi praised and thanked God.
8 Lehi named the river Laman. This river emptied into the Red Sea. The valley was near the mouth of the Red Sea.
9 Lehi saw the waters of the river. He spoke to Laman saying: “O that you might be like this river, continually springing forth righteousness!”
10 Next, Lehi spoke to Lemuel: “O that you might be like this valley, firm and steadfast, immovable in keeping the commandments of the Lord!”
