2 Nephi 31
19 “You entered the gate because of your faith in the word of the Messiah. You relied wholly on the merits of Him who is mighty to save. And now, my beloved brothers. After you begin the journey of walking in Godly ways, are you done? No, you are not done. You have come this far only because of the benevolence of the Messiah.”
20 “Wherefore, you only entered the gate. Now, you must press forward on the path. You must have a steadfastness in the Messiah. You must have a perfect brightness of hope. You must love God and men. Wherefore, if you press forward. If you feast upon the word of the Messiah. If you endure to the end. Behold, the Father will declare:” “You will have eternal life.”
