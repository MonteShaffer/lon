2 Nephi 18

1 “Next, God spoke to me (Isaiah):” “Take a large roll. Write in it with a man’s pen. Write Maher-shalal-hash-baz (Quick to the plunder, swift to the spoil).”
2 “And I (Isaiah) took with me two faithful men to witness the prophecy: Uriah the priest and Zechariah, the son of Jeberechiah.”
3 “And I knew my wife, the prophetess. She conceived and bare a son. God told me to proclaim his name to be Maher-shalal-hash-baz.”
4 “And God foretold the impending destruction of Judah. Before this son (Immanuel) has the ability to say daddy or mama, the destruction will come. Assyria will take the riches of Judah from Damascus. Assyria will take the spoil of Judah from Samaria.”
