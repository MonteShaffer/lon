2 Nephi 28
26 “Yes, wo be unto him that listens to the precepts of men! To him that denies the power of God! To him that denies the Holy Ghost!”
27 “Yes, wo unto him that says:” “We have received enough! We don’t need any more!”
28 “And in fine, wo unto all those that tremble in anger because of the truth of God! If a man has built (his worldview) on the rock (of salvation), he will receive a fullness of joy. And if a man has built (his worldview) on the sandy foundation (of the wicked one), he will tremble until the day he falls into the pit.”
29 “Wo be unto him that will say:” “We have received the word of God! We need no more of God’s word, for we have enough!”
