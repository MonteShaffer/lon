1 Nephi 16
24 And my father asked God in prayer where I should go. He and the rest of the family humbled themselves before God after hearing my rebuke. I had said many things to them with the energy of my soul.
25 And God answered my father. God chastened Lehi for murmuring. Lehi was genuinely sorry for having complained.
26 And God instructed my father to examine the Liahona: “Look at the Liahona. Behold what is written there.”
27 And my father looked. And he saw the things that were written on the Liahona. And he did fear and tremble exceedingly. And so did my brothers, the sons of Ishmael, and our wives.
28 I observed that the pointers only worked when we were faithful to God. When we were diligent and righteous. When we did not complain.
