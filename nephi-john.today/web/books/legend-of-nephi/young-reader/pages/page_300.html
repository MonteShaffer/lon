2 Nephi 23
6 “Lament for the hour has come. Jehovah will destroy the wicked on behalf of the Eternal Father.”
7 “A mighty man’s hand will slacken. His courage will melt away.”
8 “Full of fear, the enemy will look about alarmed. Anguish and sorrows will occupy their minds. They will look at one another in horror. Their faces will flush in agony. (Like a woman about to give birth.)”
9 “The day of the Lord has arrived. The day of cruelty and fury. The day of God’s fierce anger. The day of desolation. God will destroy all the sinners on the earth.”
10 “The stars of heaven and the constellations will not give off light. The sun will dim as he orbits. And the moon will not shine her light.”
11 “I will visit the world with justice for evil acts. I will punish the guilty for their crimes. The arrogance of the proud will cease. Haughty oppressors will be humiliated. ”
