2 Nephi 4
4 “God has said:” “If you keep My commandments you will prosper in the land. If you do not keep My commandments you will be cut off from My presence.”
5 “I cannot go to my grave until I bless you. You my sons and daughters. I know that if you were taught correctly, you would not depart from the truth.”
6 “I hope you are not cursed. But if you are, I bless you. I bless that the curse will be taken from you and be placed upon the heads of your parents. May they answer for this curse.”
7 “Because of my blessing, God will not allow you to perish. God will be merciful to you and your seed forever.”
8 And my father (Lehi) finished speaking to the children of Laman. Next, he asked for the children of Lemuel to be brought to him.
