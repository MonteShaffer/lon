2 Nephi 10

2 Nephi 10

1 “Now I (Jacob) speak to you again, my beloved brothers. I speak to you about the righteous branch of the house of Israel.”
2 “Behold, God’s promises we have obtained will be fulfilled in the flesh. It has been shown unto me that many of our children will perish in the flesh because of unbelief. Nevertheless, God will be merciful to many. Our children will be restored. They will come to that which will give them the true knowledge of their Redeemer.”
3 “As I have said before. It is expedient that Yeshua should come among the Jews. (Last night, an angel told me this is the Messiah’s name.) He will be among the more wicked part of the world. They will crucify Him. God finds it ironic that this people, above all other peoples, should crucify their God.”
