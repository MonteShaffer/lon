1 Nephi 20

1 Nephi 20

1 “Listen to me and hear this, O house of Jacob. You are called the house of Israel. You have come forth out of the waters of Judah. You have come out of the waters of baptism. You swear an oath in God’s name to worship God. Yet, your are not truthful nor righteous in fulfilling this oath.”
2 “You say you are from the holy city. Yet you do not abide with God. Yes, the God of Israel who is the Lord of Hosts:”
3 “I announced these important things from the beginning. In the moment I proclaimed them, I acted without hesitation. And they came to pass.”
4 “And I did this because I knew you are obstinate. Your stubborn neck is like iron sinew you and will not turn to Me. Your brow is like brass, and you will not look up to Me.”
