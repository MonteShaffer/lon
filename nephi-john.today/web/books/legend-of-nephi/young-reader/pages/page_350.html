2 Nephi 28
22 “And others he will flatter. He will tell them what they want to hear:” “There is no hell.” “He will lie:” “I am not a wicked one, for there is no wickedness.” “In this manner he whispers in their ears. He does so until he shackles them with his awful chains and drags them to hell. The place where there is no deliverance.”
23 “Yes, they are shackled with death. They are shackled with hell. Death, hell, the wicked one, and all those seized therewith must stand before God’s throne. To be judged according to their works. Since their works shackled them to the wicked one, they must go to the place prepared for him and his followers. They will go to a lake of fire and brimstone, which is endless torment.”
24 “Therefore wo be unto him that is at ease in Zion.”
25 “Wo be unto him that cries:” “All is well!”
