2 Nephi 9
53 “Behold, how great are God’s covenants. How great are His condescensions to the children of men. Because of His greatness. Because of His benevolence. Because of His mercy. He has promised us that our seed will not utterly be destroyed in the flesh. God has promised He would preserve them. In future generations, our seed will became a righteous branch to the house of Israel.”
54 “Now my brothers, I want to tell you more. But I will wait until tomorrow. Tomorrow, I will declare the remainder of my words. Amen.”
