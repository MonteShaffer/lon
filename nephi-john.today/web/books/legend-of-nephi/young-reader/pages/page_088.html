1 Nephi 14
27 And I (Nephi) heard these things. I witness that these things are true. The angel also told me the name of the apostle of the Lamb of God. His name was John.
28 And I was told not to write down the remainder of things I saw and heard. What I have written is sufficient based on what God commanded. I emphasize that I have written only a small part of the many things which I saw.
29 I witnessed these things. I saw the things my father saw. An angel of God made them known to me.
30 I conclude talking about this vision. I saw many things while carried away in the Spirit. Even though I did not write everything I saw, everything I wrote down is true. I seal this testimony with the word Amen.
