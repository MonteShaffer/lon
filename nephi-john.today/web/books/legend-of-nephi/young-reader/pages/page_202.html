2 Nephi 5
10 And we did observe to keep the judgments, statutes, and commandments. In all things, we observed God’s commands. We observed the law of Moses.
11 And God was with us. We did prosper exceedingly. We did sow seeds. We did reap again in abundance. We began to raise flocks, herds, and animals of every kind.
12 I also brought the records written on the brass plates. I brought the Liahona that was prepared by God’s hand. It was prepared for my father as I have previously explained.
13 We began to prosper exceedingly. We multiplied in the land.
14 I also took the sword of Laban. After the manner of this sword, I made many swords. We didn’t want our enemies to find us and destroy us. [As the Jews call unbelievers Gentiles,] we began calling our enemies Lamanites. This name reminds me and my people of my first enemies: my brothers Laman and Lemeul. They hated me. They hated my children. They hated all of my people.
