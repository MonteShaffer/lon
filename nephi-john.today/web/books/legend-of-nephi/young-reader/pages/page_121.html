1 Nephi 17
52 I had said so many things to my brothers. I astonished them into silence. They stopped arguing with me. They stopped trying to get their hands on me. In fact, for several days they wouldn’t even touch me with their fingers. I I had put the fear of God into them. They feared that if they touched me, they would wither and die like a dried reed.
53 As I recognized this fear, God told me: “Stretch forth your hand and touch them. I won’t have them wither and die. I will just shake them. This will remind them I am Lord their God.”
54 And I obeyed. I reached out my hand and touched my brothers. They did not wither and die. But they did convulse.
