2 Nephi 26
25 Does God say to you: “Depart from Me!”? Behold, I answer “No.” This is what God says: “Come unto Me. Come all the ends of the earth. Yes, come procure wine and milk without money and without price.”
26 Does God command any of you to depart from out of the synagogues ? Or out of the houses of worship? Behold, I answer “No.”
27 Has God commanded any of you to not partake of His salvation? Behold, I answer “No.” God has given salvation free for all men. He has commanded His people that they should persuade all men to repent.
28 Has God commanded any of you not to partake of His goodness? Behold, I answer “No.” All men are privileged equally to receive of His goodness. Everyone is welcome.
