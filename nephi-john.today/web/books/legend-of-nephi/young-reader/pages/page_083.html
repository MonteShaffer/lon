1 Nephi 14
7 “The Lamb of God declared:” “A time will come when I will perform a great and marvelous work among the children of men. This work will be everlasting. Either a man will find the peace of life eternal or he will not. If not, it will be because of man’s blindness in his mind. The man will harden his heart. And doing so, he will deliver himself as a captive of the wicked one. This will lead to his spirititual and temporal destruction.”
8 After speaking these things, the angel asked: “Do you remember God’s covenants He made with the house of Israel?” And I answered: “Yes.”
9 And the angel said: “Look!” And I looked. I saw the great and abominable church. This church is the mother of abominations. The founder of this church is the wicked one.
