1 Nephi 21
9 “You will have peace and prosperity because of My Power. You can free prisoners, they will do you no harm. You can enlighten the ignorant that abide in the darkness. Those freed from physical and mental prisons will be nourished on the highways. These greener pastures will reside in exalted places.”
10 “They will not hunger or thirst. The heat of the day or the sun will not bother them. For the God that has mercy on them will lead them. He will guide them along springs of water.”
11 “God has declared:” “And I will make all My mountains passable. My highways will be exalted.”
12 “And I will then gather my people together O house of Israel! Some will come from far. Some will come from the north. Some will come from the west. And some will come from the land of Syene.”
