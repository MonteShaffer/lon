2 Nephi 28
19 “For the kingdom of the wicked one must shake. Those that belong to it must be stirred up to repentance. Or the wicked one will shackle them with his everlasting chains. Then, they will be stirred up to anger. And they will perish.”
20 “Behold, in that day, the wicked one will rage in the hearts of the children of men. He will stir up many in anger against that which is good.”
21 “Others the wicked one will pacify. He will lull them away into carnal security. They will say:” “All is well in Zion. Yes, Zion prospers, all is well.” “And thus, the wicked one deceives their souls. He leads them away carefully down to hell.”
