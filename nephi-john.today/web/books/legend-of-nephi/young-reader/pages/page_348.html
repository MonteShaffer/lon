2 Nephi 28
15 “Wo unto the wise! Wo unto the learned! Wo unto the rich! Wo unto those puffed up in the pride of their hearts! Wo unto all those who preach false doctrines! Wo unto all who commit whoredoms! Wo unto all who pervert God’s design! As God Almighty declares:” “Wo! Wo! Wo! Wo unto these, for they will be thrust down to hell!”
16 “Wo unto those that bend the will of the just! Wo unto those that revile against that which is good, saying it has no worth! For a day will come when God speedily visits the inhabitants of the earth. In that day, those fully ripe of iniquity will perish.”
17 “Behold, in that day, some inhabitants of the earth will not be destroyed. Those that repent of their wickedness and abominations.”
18 “Behold, in that day, the great and abominable church will tumble to the earth. Yes, the whore of all the earth. Great will be the fall of this church.”
