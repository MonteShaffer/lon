2 Nephi 24

2 Nephi 24

1 “For God will have mercy on Jacob and still elect Israel to rule in the lands. Foreigners will join them. Foreigners will attach themselves to the house of Jacob.’’
2 “And God’s people will take them in and bring them to their lands. (Yes, they will be brought from the ends of the earth. God’s people will return with them to their lands of promise.) The house of Israel will receive them as an inheritance. They will serve the house of Israel as servants and handmaids. Israel will rule over their oppressors. The captives will become the captors.”
3 “In that day, God will give them rest from pain, turmoil, and hard labor.”
4 “In that day, they will take up a common saying against the king of Babel:” “Where has the oppressor gone? How did the golden city fall?”
